To scale up the pyramid, there are two simple and effective methods that I often use:

**1. Do Alpha on a broad region, then test on a smaller region.** 
For example: if an Alpha can be submitted on GLB, it will often be effective on USA, EUR, etc. An Alpha that's effective in the USA will likely also be effective in AMR.
The opposite is often not as true, but you can still try it.

**2. Within a region, choose Datafields with a relatively high number of Alpha submissions.** 
This demonstrates that Alphas are easily generated from that DF. Of course, for the purpose of scaling up the pyramid, this is a quick and suitable solution.
