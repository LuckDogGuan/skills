### 评论 #11 (作者: SJ17125, 时间: 1年前)

If you’ve already created Alphas in the ASI region, this insight could be valuable. Based on my experience, ideas that perform well in the ASI region often translate effectively to the JPN region and vice versa, likely because JPN is part of ASI. If you haven’t explored ASI yet, you can adapt your existing ideas from the USA or GLB regions, as GLB ideas have worked particularly well for me.

For those starting fresh in the JPN region, I recommend beginning with Fundamental and Analyst datasets, as they provide robust signals and are relatively straightforward to work with. JPN also tends to have lower production correlations. Once you’ve developed a few Alphas using basic operators like  `zscore` ,  `rank` , and their time-series counterparts, you can easily extend these ideas to ASI and GLB regions for broader application.
