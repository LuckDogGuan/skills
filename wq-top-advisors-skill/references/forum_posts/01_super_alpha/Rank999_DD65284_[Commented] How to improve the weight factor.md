3. **Reduce Turnover** : Lowering your alpha’s turnover (number of trades) while maintaining its performance can help increase the weight factor. This reflects the stability and sustainability of the alpha, which is highly valued in the system.
4. **Diversity and Coverage** : Having a  **diverse set of alphas**  across multiple market segments (e.g., different sectors, regions, or factors) can boost the weight factor, as it reduces risk concentration and showcases your ability to apply multiple strategies.
