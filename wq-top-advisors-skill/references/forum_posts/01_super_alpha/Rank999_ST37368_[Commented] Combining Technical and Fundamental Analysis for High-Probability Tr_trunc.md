# Combining Technical and Fundamental Analysis for High-Probability Trades

## 帖子正文

Integrating technical and fundamental analysis can significantly enhance trading outcomes.

1. **Fundamental Analysis** : Begin by identifying undervalued or overvalued assets using metrics like earnings, revenue growth, or macroeconomic factors. This provides insight into  *what*  to trade.
2. **Technical Analysis** : Use price charts, patterns, and indicators (e.g., moving averages, RSI) to determine  *when*  to trade, focusing on entry and exit points.

This hybrid approach aligns long-term value with short-term timing, increasing success probabilities. Regular backtesting and refining strategies ensure consistent and effective performance. Combining both methods balances precision with a broader market perspective.
