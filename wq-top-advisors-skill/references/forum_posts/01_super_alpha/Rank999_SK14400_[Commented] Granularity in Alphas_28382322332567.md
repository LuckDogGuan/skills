## 帖子正文

Granularity focuses on the atomic level of trading signals or predictive insights within an alpha strategy.

- **Micro-Alpha Construction:**  Break down signals into the finest granules (e.g., stock-level insights from industry trends) to identify unique drivers of performance.
- **Hierarchical Aggregation:**  Combine micro-signals into meso- and macro-level alphas (e.g., sector or market-wide signals) to diversify and strengthen the strategy.
- **Granule Clustering:**  Group similar granules using machine learning techniques (like clustering algorithms) to detect patterns or co-movements.
- **Cross-Granule Interaction:**  Analyze how individual granules interact, as correlations or conflicts may lead to overfitting or redundancies.
