# A few beginner resources for Quantitative Finance

## 帖子正文

During my journey learning about Quantitative Finance over the years, I found some free resources for beginners which have helped me gain a better understanding of it. I am listing them out here in the hope that others, especially beginners, will find them useful!

- **Quantopian Lecture Series:** These  [lectures](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRFLF1OxMm_UL7WUWM31iynp0jMVf_vLW)  are somewhat old, but cover the basics in Python and Jupyter Notebooks. Please be aware that the notebooks will not run as the Quantopian platform is now not freely available and also the data.
- **Quant Radio AI Podcasts:** These new AI hosted  [podcasts](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRFLF1OxMm_W138bt-oIqm-iQihukiJ0v)  are short, precise and cover a lot of basics in a conversational way. I especially like  ["3 Types of Backtests"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=V8OJbb_zclE&list=PLRFLF1OxMm_W138bt-oIqm-iQihukiJ0v&index=40) ,  ["The Science and Art of Predictions"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ux-QB0KdoYA&list=PLRFLF1OxMm_W138bt-oIqm-iQihukiJ0v&index=38) , and  ["Art and Science of Backtesting"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xYrfjdlvv8M&list=PLRFLF1OxMm_W138bt-oIqm-iQihukiJ0v&index=15) .
- **NPTEL Lectures:** Some good University lecture series as taught in Indian Institutes of Technology, freely available. These are long and can be boring sometimes, but your perseverance will be rewarded!  [NOC: Quantitative Finance,](https://archive.nptel.ac.in/courses/110/104/110104066/)   [NOC: Mathematical Finance](https://archive.nptel.ac.in/courses/111/103/111103126/)  and  [NOC :Quantitative Investment Management](https://archive.nptel.ac.in/courses/110/107/110107162/)  are what I had referred to.

Please share similar free resources and your valuable feedback. Let us learn together!

### 评论 #26 (作者: 顾问 PN39025 (Rank 87), 时间: 1年前)

Quantitative Finance (Quant) utilizes mathematical, statistical, and computational methods to address and solve complex financial problems. It plays a pivotal role in various fields, including trading, portfolio optimization, risk management, and derivative pricing. Below are some recommended resources to guide your learning and exploration of this field:

1. **"Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives" by John C. Hull**
   - A foundational book that explains derivatives, stochastic models, and risk management in depth.
2. **"Quantitative Risk Management: Concepts, Techniques, and Tools" by Alexander J. McNeil et al.**
   - A comprehensive resource for understanding and applying risk measurement and modeling techniques.
3. **"Algorithmic Trading: Winning Strategies and Their Rationale" by Ernest P. Chan**
   - A focused guide on developing systematic and algorithmic trading strategies.
4. **"Paul Wilmott Introduces Quantitative Finance" by Paul Wilmott**
   - This book covers essential mathematical concepts and modeling techniques used in quantitative finance.
5. **"Machine Learning for Asset Managers" by Marcos López de Prado**
   - An insightful resource bridging machine learning applications with portfolio management strategies.

These resources provide a mix of theoretical foundations and practical applications, making them ideal for both beginners and those looking to deepen their expertise in quantitative finance.

Good luck with your research!
