### **Working in Atom Format & Choosing Datasets**

✅  **Atom Format Basics**

- Atoms are simple,  **single-dataset**  alphas with minimal operators.
- They must be  **efficient** ,  **low-complexity** , and  **interpretably structured** .
- Focus on using  **fewer operators**  and avoiding  **overfitting** .

✅  **Best Datasets for Atom Alphas** 
1️⃣  **Price-Volume Data**  – Use  **close, volume, turnover, VWAP, returns** .
2️⃣  **Analyst & Model Data**  – Sentiment scores, earnings estimates.
3️⃣  **Options Data**  – Implied volatility, put-call ratios.
4️⃣  **Fundamental Data**  – Revenue growth, book-to-market ratio.
