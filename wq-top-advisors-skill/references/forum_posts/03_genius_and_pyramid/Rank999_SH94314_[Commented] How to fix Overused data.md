### 评论 #1 (作者: LN92324, 时间: 1年前)

This situation occurs when you submit an alpha that is unbalanced between categories in a region. More specifically, in the image you provided, you are submitting too much alpha in the fundamental category, so the system has blocked you from submitting alpha. The solution is to submit more alpha in other categories, then the alpha ratio between your categories will be more balanced and then you can submit alpha using fundamental datasets as usual.

### 评论 #13 (作者: KP26017, 时间: 1年前)

1. - If you're restricted from creating a pyramid in the Fundamental category for the Asia region due to overuse, here are some strategies to work around the issue:
   ### 1.  **Rebalance Your Usage**
   - **Focus on Other Categories:**  Create and simulate alphas in other categories within the Asia region (e.g., technical, sentiment-based, or macroeconomic).
   - **Rebalance Ratios:**  This helps rebalance the distribution of your alpha usage in the region, gradually unlocking access to the Fundamental category.
   ### 2.  **Wait for the Restriction Period to Expire**
   - Overuse restrictions are often temporary. Check the platform's rules or reach out to support to understand how long the restriction lasts.
   ### 3.  **Optimize Existing Alphas and Pyramids**
   - Instead of creating new alphas, optimize or resubmit existing ones in different regions or categories. This keeps you active while waiting for restrictions to reset.
   ### 4.  **Participate in Other Regions**
   - Explore alpha creation opportunities in other regions or categories not currently restricted for you. This can diversify your portfolio and maintain progress.
