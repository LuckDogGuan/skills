## 讨论与评论 (1)

### 评论 #1 (作者: HN45379, 时间: 8个月前)

Good question — for AI-based competitions, alphas that  **blend statistical depth with interpretability**  tend to perform best. Signals built from  **nonlinear operators**  (like  `power` ,  `ts_corr` , or  `arc_sin` ) often stand out, especially when they combine multiple datasets such as fundamentals, sentiment, or volatility. The key is to design expressions that show  **original logic and consistent OS behavior** , not just high in-sample Sharpe.
If you’re preparing for the upcoming round, try focusing on  **cross-dataset relationships**  or  **adaptive decays**  — they align well with the competition’s “AI meets quant intuition” theme. What type of datasets are you planning to explore?
