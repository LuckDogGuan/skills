- **链接**: [Commented] Neutralization Level Choice.md

## 帖子正文

While building valuation + sentiment alphas, I noticed results change meaningfully depending on whether I  **neutralize at sector vs subindustry level** .

Subindustry neutralization seems to:

- Reduce noise
- Improve signal purity
- But sometimes weaken macro effects

When working with valuation factors, do you prefer  **sector** ,  **subindustry** , or  **no neutralization at all** and why?
