### 评论 #2 (作者: CM46415, 时间: 2个月前)

For beginners, start with simple, well-understood datasets like price/volume data (returns, close, volume, turnover) and basic fundamentals if available. These are stable and easier to interpret.

For operators, focus on core building blocks like ts_rank, ts_mean, ts_delta, rank, scale, and zscore since they help you understand signal construction clearly.

Model77 and PV1 can be included once you’re comfortable, especially for structured portfolio-style signals and value-based construction.
