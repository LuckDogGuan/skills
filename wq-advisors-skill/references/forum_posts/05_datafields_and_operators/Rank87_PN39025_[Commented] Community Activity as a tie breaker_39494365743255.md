### 评论 #5 (作者: CM46415, 时间: 2个月前)

Community activity on BRAIN is usually driven by  **consistent, meaningful engagement** , not just volume.

Key ways to improve it:

- **Post regularly** : share insights, alpha learnings, or experiments instead of occasional long posts
- **Engage with others** : meaningful comments, feedback, and discussions increase visibility more than isolated posts
- **Be useful** : explain operators, debug ideas, or share frameworks (like your pipeline-style posts)
- **Consistency matters** : daily/weekly activity builds stronger ranking signals than bursts
- **Quality over spam** : low-value or repetitive posts don’t help much and may be ignored by the algorithm

In practice, treat it like signal research:  **clarity, usefulness, and consistency compound over time** .
