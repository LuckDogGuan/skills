Mapping the idea to data can make your alphas distinctive. Powered by advanced AI technologies, such as NLP, The  [**Data Explorer**](https://platform.worldquantbrain.com/data)  helps you quickly and accurately identify the data fields to implement your idea.

## Searching for fields based on an idea

Let’s say you have an alpha idea using market sentiment. You can easily search for related datasets and data fields in the Data Explorer. Notice that you will have to pre-define region, delay and universe prior to your search.

## Searching for fields based on specific criteria

You can filter data fields based on criteria such as coverage, type, count of alphas and users. For example, you can search for high coverage data fields from  **Company Fundamental Data for Equity** dataset in CHN region and were used in at least 5 submitted alphas. You can later sort these data fields by alpha count to see which fields are used less, i.e., less crowded.

Don’t limit yourself to these usages, there are many more way to utilize this feature.
