When it comes to  **feature engineering**  for alpha generation, the main expectation is that you can extract  *meaningful, tradable signals*  from the raw datasets — not just plug the data directly into formulas. In other words, you’re expected to transform the datasets in ways that expose underlying market behavior or statistical structure.

### 评论 #20 (作者: AG14039, 时间: 6个月前)

When doing feature engineering for alpha generation, the key expectation is that you can transform raw datasets into meaningful, tradeable signals rather than simply feeding the data directly into formulas. The goal is to apply operations that reveal underlying market patterns, structural relationships, or statistical behaviors that aren’t obvious in the raw fields. In essence, the value comes from how effectively you shape and refine the data to extract genuine predictive insight.
