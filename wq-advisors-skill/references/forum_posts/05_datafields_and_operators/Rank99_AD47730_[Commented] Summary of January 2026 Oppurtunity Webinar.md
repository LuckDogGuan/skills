## 帖子正文

The session focused on  **new research opportunities and recent performance highlights across regions** .

**1. Country-Specific Research Themes**

- A new  **Country-Specific (CS) theme**  is introduced for AMR, TWN, HKG, JPN, and KOR.
- Alphas must maintain  **at least 70% of original performance** , with enhanced max-trade testing (especially for ASI regions).
- More datasets are now available, expanding research scope.
- **Start date:**  12 Jan 2026
- **Multiplier:**  2× for both Regular Alphas and ATOMs.

**2. Dataset Opportunity Insights**

- Across regions,  **MODEL and PV datasets**  dominate alpha usage.
- Fundamental and Analyst datasets also play a meaningful role, while other categories contribute in smaller proportions.
- The mix varies by region, highlighting the importance of region-specific strategies.

**3. Performance Highlights**

- **IND region theme**  showed strong and consistent cumulative performance over time.
- **AIAC submissions**  also demonstrated steady growth.
- Key performance stats showed healthy Sharpe ratios, solid returns, and controlled turnover.

**4. Top Dataset Categories Driving Results**

- For IND themes: PV and MODEL datasets led performance.
- For AIAC submissions: PV, MODEL, RISK, and ANALYST datasets were the main contributors.

**5.**  In the End Announcement for Monthly Power Pool and AIAC Competetion was held. With total of 80 winners in Power Pool and 10 for AIAC
