## 帖子正文

I have been exploring how to effectively create signals that capture market overreaction and underreaction without introducing overfitting. One approach is to use news or sentiment data to identify hyped stocks or products that attract excessive investor attention. These assets often experience sharp price increases during initial excitement, followed by significant declines once the hype fades and investors exit their positions.

Lets take Trump coin from crypto world as example, it experienced a temporary surge in price during its lauch driven by speculation and attention, followed by a rapid drop as sentiment normalized.

However, my attempts to implement this using functions like  *if_else*  and  *trade_when*  have mainly been effective only in U.S. and Asian markets, with limited success in other regions.
