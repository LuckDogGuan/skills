# 🚀 Some Best Practices for PowerPool Alphas

## 帖子正文

If you’re working on PowerPool Alphas, here are a few important things to keep in mind:

1. **Build on solid logic**  – Always make sure your Alpha comes from strong economic reasoning. Don’t just throw ideas at the wall—understand  *why*  it should work
2. **No API mining**  – Avoid generating Alphas automatically through APIs. The quality matters much more than quantity here.
3. **Understand what you submit**  – Even though we keep the submission rules pretty flexible, please don’t submit Alphas you don’t actually understand. If you can’t explain how it works, it probably doesn’t belong in PowerPool.
4. **Use tags for diversity**  – Tag your Alphas thoughtfully so we can maintain variety. For example, don’t put in multiple Alphas based on the exact same dataset or idea.
5. **Think of the bigger picture**  – Power Pool is a great way to balance and strengthen your overall performance. By adding diversity to your pool, you increase your chances of achieving more consistent results.

👉 In short: focus on quality, understanding, and diversity. PowerPool can be a powerful tool—use it wisely, and it’ll reward you!
