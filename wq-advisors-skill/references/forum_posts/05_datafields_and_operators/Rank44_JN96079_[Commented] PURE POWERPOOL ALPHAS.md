## 讨论与评论 (10)

### 评论 #1 (作者: KL44463, 时间: 5个月前)

A pure PPA is an alpha that meets the Power Pool requirements in WQ Brain but does not satisfy the Regular Alpha criteria (for example, fitness > 1 or 2-year Sharpe > 1.58). It does not need to pass self-correlation or product correlation checks and is only evaluated against Power Pool correlation.

Under the new WQ Brain policy, only pure PPAs that fit a Power Pool theme can be submitted, which currently applies to the IND region.

### 评论 #7 (作者: 顾问 JN96079 (Rank 44), 时间: 5个月前)

In WQ Brain, a pure PPA is an alpha that clears the Power Pool entry rules but doesn’t meet the stricter standards required for a Regular Alpha, such as a minimum fitness level or a two-year Sharpe above 1.58. These alphas are exempt from self-correlation and production-correlation checks and are evaluated solely on their correlation within the Power Pool. Under the latest policy update, only pure PPAs that align with an active Power Pool theme are eligible for submission. At present, that effectively means pure PPAs are limited to those built for the IND region.
