### 评论 #2 (作者: JO96892, 时间: 2个月前)

- Apart from diversification move away or submit less in regions that do not give you best results.
- Also prioritize regions that might have been discussed in the webinars and advised by your supervisor.
- Lastly, stop chasing pyramids blindly. More often researchers tend to fill up pyramids in hurry at the last weeks of the quarter so as to meet their targets, but when this is not done accordingly it might affect you!

### 评论 #3 (作者: YD84002, 时间: 2个月前)

First, aim to submit alphas above 20 in this region — that seems like a solid benchmark. But one thing to keep in mind: transaction fees in Asia are different from other regions, so don't just copy-paste strategies from the US or Europe without adjusting for costs.

Also, try to keep your MAR (Minimum Acceptable Return) above 10%. That gives you a nice buffer even after accounting for the higher fees.
