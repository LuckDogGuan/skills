## 帖子正文

After exploring both community discussions and the official Osmosis guide, here’s what I understood about Daily Osmosis Rank:

Daily Osmosis Rank is not a direct alpha quality score.
It’s based on 1-day PnL of your Osmosis combo, so short-term fluctuations are mostly noise (Based on allocations from 2 weeks ago).

It directly impacts your payments as it acts as a multiplier (1 to 2) on your daily base payment so better rank may give higher payouts.

**Why daily fluctuations are completely normal**

- Rank depends on one-day out-of-sample PnL
- Small changes in alpha behavior implies large rank swings

**Allocation strategy of mine** :

- Diversify across regions, datasets, operators
- Prioritize consistent, low-cost alphas
- Focus on orthogonality, not just high Sharpe
