## 讨论与评论 (6)

### 评论 #1 (作者: TP85668, 时间: 6个月前)

In the  **Osmosis**  competition,  **combo expressions (SuperAlphas)**  often carry more weight  *if*  they are truly  **diverse, stable, and low-correlation** , since scoring emphasizes aggregated contribution and uniqueness. That said,  **ATOM alphas**  can still be highly weighted when they represent  **clean, pure, and non-overlapping signals** . In short, it’s not atom vs combo—the real driver is  **how independent and valuable the signal is to the overall pool** .
