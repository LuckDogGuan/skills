### 评论 #28 (作者: RP41479, 时间: 1年前)

Combined Selected Alpha Performance is a subset of Combined Alpha Performance. If your Combined Selected Alpha Performance is lower, it means the alphas chosen by WorldQuant are underperforming relative to your full pool; if higher, they are stronger. Therefore, improving your Combined Alpha Performance is key to boosting both selection quality and earnings.

To enhance Combined Alpha Performance, start by managing turnover. Monitor both average and maximum daily turnover, and use turnover plots to reduce peaks. Operators like  `tradewhen` ,  `hump` ,  `ts_target_tvr_delta_limit` , and  `ts_delta_limit`  help in maintaining smoother turnover.

Next, ensure your alpha weights are balanced across various capitalization sizes. Use visual tools to avoid biases toward large-cap or small-cap stocks.

High universe coverage is also critical. Aim for your long and short positions combined to cover at least 50% of the universe, and focus on liquid stocks. Avoid strong short bias where shorts greatly outnumber longs.

Finally, pay close attention to sub-universe performance. Do not rely on alphas that barely pass these tests. Instead, use Universe dataset fields to build custom sub-universe filters for better robustness.

Applying these practices will improve your alpha pool's performance, increase its production viability, and strengthen your overall ranking.
