## 帖子正文

### **1. Low Turnover**

- **Why Prefer** :
  - Reduces transaction costs and slippage.
  - Increases net profitability.
  - Makes the alpha more stable and scalable.

### **2. High Fitness**

- **Why Prefer** :
  - Combines Sharpe, turnover, and consistency.
  - A strong overall indicator of alpha quality.
  - Helps prioritize which alphas to deploy or combine.

### **3. High Sharpe Ratio**

- **Why Prefer** :
  - Shows better return per unit of risk.
  - Reflects efficient use of volatility.
  - Indicates a more robust and reliable signal.

### **4. Low Drawdown**

- **Why Prefer** :
  - Reduces the chance of major capital loss.
  - Improves risk control and investor confidence.
  - Allows smoother compounding of returns.

### **5. High Returns**

- **Why Prefer** :
  - Demonstrates actual profitability.
  - Essential for alpha contribution in a portfolio.
  - Increases overall alpha strength when combined with low risk.
