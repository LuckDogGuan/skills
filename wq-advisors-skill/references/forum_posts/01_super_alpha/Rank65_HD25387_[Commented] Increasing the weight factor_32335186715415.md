## ⚖️  **What Is the Weight Factor in WorldQuant Brain (WQB)?**

**Weight Factor**  reflects how much your  **Alphas are used in actual production portfolios**  by WorldQuant’s internal portfolio managers (PMs). It is  *not just a performance score* , but a measure of  **real-world utility** .

Even a  **small positive weight**  means  **your Alpha is contributing to real trading strategies** , which can:

- 🔼  **Boost your Genius rank**
- 💰  **Increase your quarterly payment**
- 🎯 Indicate long-term value of your Alpha research

## 🧭  **How to Increase Your Weight Factor**

Here’s a detailed roadmap used by GrandMasters and Master consultants:

### 🔹 1.  **Target the Right Universes**

Focus on regions that are  **frequently used by PMs** :

- **USA**  (most weighted)
- **GLOBAL**
- **EUR**
- **ASI**  (moderate)

> These have the highest chance of being picked for production use.

### 🔹 2.  **Diversify Smartly**

Avoid repeating the same structure or theme. Aim for:

- Different  **data categories**  (price, volume, fundamental, short interest, estimates)
- Different  **styles**  (momentum, mean-reversion, seasonality, value)
- Different  **subuniverses**  (large-cap, small-cap, sectoral splits)

### 🔹 3.  **Improve Alpha Quality**

High-weight alphas tend to have:

- **Sharpe > 1.5**
- **Fitness > 2.5**
- **Turnover < 0.2**
- **Margin > 5bps**

Avoid:

- High-turnover alphas
- Overfitted expressions
- Highly correlated submissions

### 🔹 4.  **Join the Power Pool**

- Power Pool = 3x higher exposure to PMs
- Better chance your Alpha gets selected for  **weight testing**
- Build Power Pool Alphas using:
  - **Single dataset**
  - **Strong subuniverse Sharpe**
  - **Robust performance in IS and OS**

### 🔹 5.  **Use Advanced Techniques**

PMs look for well-constructed, risk-adjusted ideas:

- Use  **neutralization settings**  (Statistical, Fast, Slow)
- Use  **MaxTrade: ON**  for control over execution
- Layer operators like  `combo_a` ,  `ts_decay` ,  `zscore` ,  `delay` ,  `rank` ,  `scale`  to build more stable alphas

### ⏳ 6.  **Give It Time**

- Weight usually takes  **6–12 months**  to appear, because:
  - OS performance must be stable
  - Portfolio managers must review and integrate your Alpha
- Be  **consistent** —keep submitting high-quality, diverse alphas each week
