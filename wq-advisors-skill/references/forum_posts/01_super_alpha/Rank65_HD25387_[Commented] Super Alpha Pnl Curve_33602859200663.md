🚀  **Phase 2 (Accelerated Growth):** 
If the alpha's logic aligns with structural market shifts or evolving inefficiencies, a nonlinear uptrend may reflect  **compounding edge**  or improved signal relevance.

🔍  **Key Things to Validate:**

- **No Overfitting:**  Ensure the inflection isn’t due to curve-fitting or regime-specific behavior. Use SS/IS split to validate.
- **Factor Drift:**  Check if the alpha is increasingly aligning with strong macro/microeconomic factors.
- **Turnover & Drawdown:**  Make sure the rise isn’t being driven by excessive turnover or risk.
- **Subperiod Performance:**  Validate whether the upward shift is  **broad-based**  across slices or concentrated in a few segments.

💡 Bottom line:
A strong nonlinear finish  **isn't a red flag** —it can indicate rising informational efficiency of the signal. But always pair visual optimism with  **statistical caution** .

What matters most: consistency, explanation, and resilience across markets. 📊✅
