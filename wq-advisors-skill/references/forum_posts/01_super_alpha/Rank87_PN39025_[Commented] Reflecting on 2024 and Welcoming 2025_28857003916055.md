Hello everyone,

2025 is just around the corner. Our journey as alphas is about to transition into a new and promising phase. In 2024, I achieved a few small personal milestones by participating in major competitions like SAC, MAPC, and ATOM. The most exciting and highly anticipated event, as we prepare to welcome the New Year—not just for me but possibly for all of you—is GENIUS.

Over the past year, I’ve also explored and learned incredible things in the fields of  **Quantitative Research**  and  **Data Analysis** , while meeting many talented friends and mentors within the industry.

Looking ahead to 2025, there will be numerous changes in how we approach alpha generation, the way WorldQuant operates, and new competitions and challenges waiting to begin.

Wishing you all good health, success in achieving your goals, and high rankings in GENIUS as you hope for. Let’s welcome the New Year joyfully with our family and friends.

Feel free to share your experiences, challenges, and achievements from 2024 with me. Thank you so much!
