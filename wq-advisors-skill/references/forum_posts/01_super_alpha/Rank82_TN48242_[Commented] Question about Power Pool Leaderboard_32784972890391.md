### 评论 #1 (作者: CH85564, 时间: 1年前)

Yes, the  **Power Pool Leaderboard**  you see on the page is part of the new  **Power Pool Monthly Competition** , which started in  **June 2025**  and runs each calendar month (based on EST time zone).

This leaderboard  **is used for rewards** , specifically to recognize top-performing consultants based on their  **Power Pool Alpha Combo performance** . It includes filters such as "Board by region/delay" to separate results by category (USA D1, GLB D1, ASI D1, EUR D1).

**Key points:**

- **Top 10**  participants in each regional category  **will receive monetary awards** :
  - 1st–3rd:  **$500**
  - 4th–6th:  **$300**
  - 7th–10th:  **$150**
- A consultant can win in  **multiple categories** , up to a  **maximum of $2,000/month** .
- The Power Pool Monthly awards are  **separate**  from the  **base payment**  and  **quarterly payment** , which will  **continue as usual** .
- All Power Pool Alphas still  **count toward base pay, quarterly bonuses, and Genius levels** .
- The competition has  **no prod correlation** ,  **no IS ladder** ,  **no fitness threshold** , and  **lower Sharpe ratio requirements** .

So yes, this leaderboard directly relates to  **rewards and payments** , and it's an opportunity to earn additional income alongside your regular base and quarterly payments. Thats all, please like my post !!!
