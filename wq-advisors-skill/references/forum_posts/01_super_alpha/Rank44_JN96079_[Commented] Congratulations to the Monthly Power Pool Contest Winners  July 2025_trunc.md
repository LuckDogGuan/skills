### 评论 #13 (作者: NT84064, 时间: 10个月前)

Congratulations to all the winners! What really stands out in these results is the  **regional diversity of strong performers**  across USA, GLB, ASI, and EUR D1 universes. It highlights how important it is to tailor alpha research to different universes rather than relying only on global templates. For example, an operator combination that works well in GLB may not generalize to ASI due to structural differences in liquidity, volatility, and sector distribution. This is why it’s so critical to stress-test alphas in multiple universes before tagging them for contests. Another key takeaway is that successful consultants often balance  **short-horizon signals**  with  **longer smoothing operators**  to capture both microstructure noise and broader market trends. For anyone aiming to be on this list next time, focusing on  **orthogonality**  (low correlation with your own pool and the broader Genius pool) is just as important as raw Sharpe. Treat the Power Pool as a mini portfolio exercise, where robustness, diversity, and adaptability matter as much as creativity.
