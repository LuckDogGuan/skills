### 评论 #6 (作者: LB76673, 时间: 9个月前)

he PowerPool selection works on a  **quarterly cycle** , and the platform typically “freezes” your tagged PPAs shortly before the official update. In practice, if you want your alphas to be counted in the upcoming PowerPool release, you need to have the PowerPool tag set before the  **quarter-end cutoff**  (for example, June 30 for the Q2 cycle). Any changes you make after the cutoff will only apply to the following quarter’s update, even if the tag shows up in your account right away. So yes, you can still change tags now, but what matters is whether the tag is active on your alphas at the official end-of-quarter snapshot time.

### 评论 #7 (作者: SR82953, 时间: 2个月前)

The combined performance metrics are typically updated with a delay of about one month. For example, alphas submitted up to March 2026 would generally be reflected in the combined performance update around the first week of May. So, for any given cycle, only those alphas that were  **submitted and already tagged (e.g., PowerPool)**  on or before the cutoff date (like March 30, 2026 in this case) will be considered. Changing tags after that cutoff won’t impact the upcoming update, so it’s important to ensure your desired alphas are properly tagged before the end-of-month deadline.
