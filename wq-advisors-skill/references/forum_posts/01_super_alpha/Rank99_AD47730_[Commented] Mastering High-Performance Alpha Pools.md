## 帖子正文

Create stronger Power Pool Alphas with these essentials:• Check merged performance before tagging• Focus on low turnover & liquid universes• Reuse strong signals from your pool• Use Investability Constrained PNL• Diversify across ideas, datasets & operators• Remove highly correlated Alphas• Consult your research advisorStrong pools come from smart selection and diversification.
