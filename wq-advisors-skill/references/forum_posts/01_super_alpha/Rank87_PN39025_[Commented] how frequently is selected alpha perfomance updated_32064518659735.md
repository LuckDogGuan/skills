### 评论 #3 (作者: TP18957, 时间: 1年前)

Selected Alpha Performance is typically updated once your alpha has been selected into the firm's internal strategies, which depends not just on performance but also on factors like orthogonality, stability, and universe coverage. Unlike Combined Alpha Performance—which aggregates all your submissions—Selected Alpha Performance reflects actual usage in production-level models. It may take several months (often 4–12) before your alphas are chosen, especially if you're relatively new to the platform. During that time, focusing on improving your alpha diversity, reducing overfitting, and submitting consistently can improve selection chances. If it still shows as N/A, it likely means no alpha has been used in production yet—but that can change over time.
