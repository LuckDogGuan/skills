### 评论 #2 (作者: MY82844, 时间: 5个月前)

**Monthly Power Pool Competition**  is being replaced with  **short, focused Thematic Power Pool competitions** .

- Competitions may run over shorter periods and focus on specific themes (e.g. IND region 5 Jan - 18 Jan).
- Only  **Power Pool Alphas that match the theme’s research direction**  are counted in that competition.
- Pure Power Pool Alphas can be submitted only within Thematic Power Pool Competition.
