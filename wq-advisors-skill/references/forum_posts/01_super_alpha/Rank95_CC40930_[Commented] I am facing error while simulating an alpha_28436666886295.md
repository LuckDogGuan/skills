### 评论 #9 (作者: DN41247, 时间: 1年前)

It seems like your issue might be caused by one or a combination of the following reasons:

1. **Vector Data Usage** : Ensure you are using vector operators correctly before working with vector datasets. This converts vector data into matrix data, which is essential for proper simulations.
2. **Alpha Complexity** : If your Alpha is too complex or includes a large number of component Alphas, it could significantly slow down simulation time or even cause errors. Try simplifying the design or breaking it down into smaller components.
3. **Platform Congestion** : The Brain platform might be experiencing peak-hour congestion, especially if many consultants are working simultaneously. If this is the case, consider trying during off-peak hours or raising a support ticket to alert the Tech Team.
4. **Network Stability** : Double-check your internet connection to ensure it’s stable and not causing interruptions during simulations.
5. **General Errors** : Sometimes, the error messages returned might not directly specify the problem, so it’s good to check for broader issues like invalid simulation logic or unsupported operations.

I recommend starting by validating your vector data handling and simplifying your Alpha. If the problem persists, raising a ticket with the platform's support team might expedite resolution.
