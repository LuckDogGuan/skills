### 评论 #5 (作者: DJ40095, 时间: 1年前)

To improve your OS score in the Super Alpha Competition 2025 (SAC), focus on submitting only eligible SuperAlphas—they must use Delay 1, belong to the USA, ASI, EUR, or GLOBAL universes, and include component Alphas from only one category as announced each week. Make sure to submit at least 10 qualified SuperAlphas during the competition to be eligible for final rankings, and at least 2 per week to qualify for weekly prizes.

Each week focuses on a different Alpha category, so align your submissions accordingly. Build high-quality, uncorrelated Alphas with strong In-Sample (IS) and Out-of-Sample (OOS) metrics, and avoid overfitting. When constructing SuperAlphas, combine diverse and effective Alphas to create a balanced portfolio, as scores are based on the equal-weighted average of all your valid SuperAlphas.

Stay consistent throughout the six-week competition, as scores reset weekly—this gives you fresh chances to rank well even if a previous week wasn’t successful. Lastly, avoid disqualification by using only one BRAIN account, not sharing credentials, and ensuring all your profile information is accurate and compliant with the rules.
