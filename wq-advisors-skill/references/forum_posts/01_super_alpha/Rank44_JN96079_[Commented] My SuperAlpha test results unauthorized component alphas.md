### 评论 #2 (作者: PM26052, 时间: 6个月前)

This usually happens when your SuperAlpha references one or more component alphas that aren’t allowed in the current competition or sandbox—either they’re private, unsubmitted, or restricted. Cloning the alpha and re-simulating ensures only authorized components are included.
