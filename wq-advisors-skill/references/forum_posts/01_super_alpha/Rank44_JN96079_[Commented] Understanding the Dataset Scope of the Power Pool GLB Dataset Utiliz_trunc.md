## 帖子正文

Power Pool usually works best when performance comes from combining many diverse, weak signals. With this theme, the allowed dataset list feels pretty narrow, which makes true diversification harder.

Maybe the goal is deeper feature engineering within these datasets rather than broad mixing, but that’s a shift from the usual Power Pool idea.

Curious how others are approaching this. Are you focusing on transformations and horizons to create diversity, or treating this as a constrained feature-extraction challenge?
