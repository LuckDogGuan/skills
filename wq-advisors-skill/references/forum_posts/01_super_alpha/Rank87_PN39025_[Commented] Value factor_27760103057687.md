### 评论 #8 (作者: 顾问 PN39025 (Rank 87), 时间: 1年前)

The Value Factor is primarily determined by the OS performance of your most recently submitted Alphas during the applicable calendar quarter and is not directly tied to specific regions. However, diversifying your submissions—across Alpha ideas, regions, and settings—can significantly enhance your Value Factor. By incorporating varied datasets and market environments, you increase the likelihood of identifying high-performing Alphas, thereby improving your overall score.

### Example:

Suppose your submissions have mostly focused on North American equities. While these might perform well in certain quarters, a lack of diversity could limit your Value Factor if market conditions shift. By expanding to include Alphas derived from Asian or European datasets, or by applying alternative strategies, you tap into new opportunities and hedge against region-specific volatility. This balanced approach can improve both Alpha performance and your Value Factor.
