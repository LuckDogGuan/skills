# PROPOSAL:ENHANCED WELCOME KIT FOR NEW CONSULTANTS

## 帖子正文

To enhance onboarding experience for new ( **Kenyan** ) consultants, I propose a revamped merchandise and enhanced logistics for a much greater impact. The following proposal reimagines the welcome kit by offering an alternative to the current WQB branded cap with a  **T-shirt** (or offering both if possible).
The current plan, whilst being good, can even be made better. The redesign aims to focus on  ***brand visibility*** ,  ***consultant preference*** and   ***cost effective logistics* .**

 **Design Proposal for the T-Shirt

 
> [!NOTE] [图片 OCR 识别内容]
> BRAIN
> UoN
** The Front of the T-shirt should contain the  ***BRAIN*** logo and the  ***university*** of the consultant as provided in the  ***mockup*** design sample above.
>> Including the  ***university name*** on the T-shirt fosters a stronger  ***sense of belonging*** and  ***pride*** among new consultants. It creates a  ***visual connection*** to their  ***academic community*** , boosting morale and promoting WQB within university campuses.

The back of the T-shirt
 
> [!NOTE] [图片 OCR 识别内容]
> Life =
> (Ups*Downs);
> ts_moment(Life; c
> C
 The message at the back should feature an  ***engaging*** and  ***motivational*** phrase in " ***WQB Language* "** . This not only spreads  ***GOOD VIBES*** but also demystifies the WQB identity whilst creating a  ***positive*** and  **approachable** image.

As is in the case above; " *Life is a product of ups and downs. Over time, every moment shapes the journey. Embrace both, for in the infinite horizon, they define resilience, growth, and meaning. Keep going!"* 

**Why a T-shirt ?**  **Greater Appeal;** T-shirts are worn more frequently as compared to caps and  are also more preferred

 ***Brand Visibility** ;* Current items lack campus-centric branding. The T-shirts can be customized for campuses. Wearing the T-shirt on campus will act as a form of organic marketing, increasing brand awareness and potentially attracting new talent to WQB.

 **THE LOGISTICS** To ensure  **cost effectiveness** and  **consultant satisfaction,** below are some brainstormed ideas;
**Deliveries should be done at the end of  the quarter, as opposed to the previous mode where it was ad-hoc. The number of onboarding consultants will be certain for each university.
**Get volunteers from various campuses to act as distributors. In the event there are more than one volunteer per campus ,consultants can vote/select one {or work with both if possible}
**The vendor should get in touch with these volunteers and deliver the merchandise to them in bulk as opposed to the previous mode where the vendor would do deliveries to each person  individually.
**The new consultants should get in touch with the volunteers and pick the merchandise in preselected areas (preferably around campus) or make convenient arrangements.
**The consultants  should select their sizes( ***S*** , **M** , ***L*** , ***XL*** or  ***XXL to guarantee a comfortable fit*** ) via  a prompt email and the details shared to the vendor. and the campus volunteers for confirmation upon collection.

This proposal enhances WQB’s welcome kit by prioritizing consultant preferences, strengthening brand visibility, and streamlining logistics. By investing in merchandise that resonates with young professionals, WQB will foster loyalty and position itself as an employer of choice in Kenya’s/GLOBAL competitive talent market.
 **Let’s empower our consultants to wear their pride!**
