# Why High Turnover, Low Sharpe, and Big Drawdowns Kill Alphas

## 帖子正文

### **1. High Turnover (Not Preferred)**

- **Why Not** :
  - Leads to high transaction costs and slippage.
  - Reduces net returns.
  - Increases noise and trading instability.

### **2. Low Fitness**

- **Why Not** :
  - Indicates poor overall quality.
  - Often tied to noisy signals, high costs, or low returns.
  - Weak candidates for live deployment.

### **3. Low Sharpe Ratio**

- **Why Not** :
  - High risk with little return.
  - Suggests poor risk-adjusted performance.
  - May be unprofitable after trading costs.

### **4. High Drawdown**

- **Why Not** :
  - Signals large or frequent losses.
  - Can trigger capital allocation cuts or stop-outs.
  - Damages investor trust and portfolio stability.

### **5. Low Returns**

- **Why Not** :
  - Weak alpha signal.
  - Even if low risk, contributes little value.
  - Can be overwhelmed by trading costs or noise.
