### 评论 #7 (作者: TD17989, 时间: 1年前)

**Alpha**  refers to the  **excess returns**  generated by an investment or trading strategy over and above the returns predicted by a benchmark (such as the market or specific factors like size or value from the Fama-French model). In other words, alpha is the performance that cannot be explained by the overall market movements or common risk factors.
