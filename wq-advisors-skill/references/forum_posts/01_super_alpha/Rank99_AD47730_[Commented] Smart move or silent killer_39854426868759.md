---

## 讨论与评论 (7)

### 评论 #1 (作者: CM46415, 时间: 2个月前)

Reusing the same selection or combo expression in a super alpha increases redundancy, raises internal correlation, reduces diversification benefits, weakens marginal contribution, and may lower overall pool performance robustness.

---

### 评论 #2 (作者: EM39360, 时间: 2个月前)
