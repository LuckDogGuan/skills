**Update: Revised Simulation Limits for GLB Region**

To ensure a smoother simulation experience and maintain system performance, updated the  **maximum number of concurrent simulations**  allowed in the  **GLB region** .

**New Limits:**

- **4 concurrent simulations**  for  **GLB Alphas**
- **1 concurrent simulation**  for  **GLB SuperAlphas**

What this means for you:
These adjustments are designed to  **improve simulation speed and stability** , helping you test your ideas more efficiently without overloading the system.

Keep building, keep testing, and may your simulations run faster than ever!
