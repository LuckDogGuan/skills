### 评论 #12 (作者: SD92473, 时间: 9个月前)

At the Master and Grandmaster levels, there isn’t a single “ideal” tiebreaker value — it’s more about balance and consistency. Generally, keeping  **operators per alpha**  in a moderate range (not overly simple, but not unnecessarily complex) tends to work best. For  **fields per alpha** , 2–4 is often enough to capture signal without overfitting. In terms of overall  **operators and fields used** , diversity matters more than raw counts — covering multiple operator families and datasets gives stronger, more robust performance. The key is variety with discipline, not just inflating numbers.
