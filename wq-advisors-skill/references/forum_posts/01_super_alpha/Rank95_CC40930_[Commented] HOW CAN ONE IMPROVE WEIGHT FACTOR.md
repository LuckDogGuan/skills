**Increasing the Weight Factor requires patience and consistency, often taking 9 months to 1 year. To achieve this, follow these steps:**

By consistently applying these strategies, your Weight Factor will gradually improve, significantly enhancing the impact of your alphas over time.

**Weight** represents how well your alpha generates profits when invested. Therefore, it requires 6-9 months to calculate its score.

If the Mega Super Alpha is invested and performs well, everyone involved will be rewarded with Weight points.

To increase your Weight, you should focus on creating alphas that:

- Have low correlation (to increase the likelihood of being selected).
- Are diversified across multiple regions and datasets (to increase both the selection rate and quantity).
- Are strong, clear, and well-conceived (to perform well in the OS and become a "key" component of the Mega Super Alpha). Previously, I saw someone in the Weight leaderboard submit just one alpha and earn 3k Weight points.
