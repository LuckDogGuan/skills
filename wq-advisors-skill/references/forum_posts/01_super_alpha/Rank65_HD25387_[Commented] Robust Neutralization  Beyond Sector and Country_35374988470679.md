## 帖子正文

Sector/country neutralization is common, but I’ve experimented with:

- **Liquidity neutralization**  (ADV, turnover) → helps prevent alphas from being liquidity-biased.
- **Volatility neutralization**  → reduces drawdowns in crisis periods.
- **Custom grouping (market_cap buckets)**  → balances small vs. large cap exposures.

Sometimes neutralization removes the very edge (e.g., momentum alphas often  *need*  liquidity bias). The trick is to test  **partial neutralization** , rather than full.

👉 Question: Do you ever experiment with non-standard neutralization factors?
