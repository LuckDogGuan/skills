## 帖子正文

**Power Pool submission rules**

- Submissions must contain no more than  **8 total operators** , including repeats.
- The  **sub-universe check**  will now be applied to submissions.

**Reminders:**

- Ensure your  **Alpha descriptions**  are accurate and high-quality to maintain eligibility.
- More Alphas doesn’t always mean better performance. Be selective.
- **Untagged Alphas**  will still remain in the self-correlation pool.
