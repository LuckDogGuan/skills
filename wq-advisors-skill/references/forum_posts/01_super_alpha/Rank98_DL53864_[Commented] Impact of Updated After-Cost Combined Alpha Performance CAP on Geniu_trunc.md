After the recent update to the  **After-Cost Combined Alpha Performance (CAP)** :

- Out of  **2,403 consultants**  (who have submitted at least one signal this quarter),  **156 consultants**  have a CAP of  **≥ 2** , making them eligible for the  **Grandmaster level** , provided they meet other criteria like Signal Creation and Pyramid Completion.
- Similarly,  **774 consultants**  (156 + 618) have a CAP of  **≥ 1** , qualifying them for the  **Master level** .

Breaking it down further:

- Of the current  **31 Grandmasters** , only  **11**  have a CAP of  **≥ 2** , meaning they remain eligible for Grandmaster status under the updated CAP.
- For the  **121 Masters** ,  **86 consultants**  (29 + 57) meet the CAP requirement of  **≥ 1**  to retain their Master level status.

This update impacts every Genius level, reflecting the importance of maintaining high CAP scores to secure or advance in ranking.

*Note: This analysis only includes the 2,403 consultants who submitted at least one signal this quarter.*

### 评论 #6 (作者: DP11917, 时间: 1年前)

- **Gross Alpha** : This is the return generated by a strategy before considering any costs.
- **Costs** : This includes transaction fees (brokerage, spreads, etc.), slippage (the difference between the expected price of a trade and the actual price), market impact, and possibly other operational costs.
- **After-Cost Alpha** : Once the above costs are subtracted from the gross alpha, you get the after-cost alpha, which reflects the real-world performance of the strategy.
