**Note** : Higher-level users are reassessed each quarter. This means maintaining a high rank is not guaranteed unless consistent performance is achieved. However, users at higher levels benefit from greater stakes compared to others.

   Submit alphas regularly and keep your simulation streak alive. Genius programs reward sustained efforts.
5. **Engage with the Community** :
   Participate in forums and discussions to exchange ideas and gain valuable insights that could refine your approach.
