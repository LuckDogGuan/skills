### 评论 #10 (作者: TP14664, 时间: 1年前)

- **Alpha** : Refers to the excess return generated by the strategy over the benchmark. A positive alpha is desirable as it indicates that the strategy is outperforming the market (or the benchmark) after adjusting for risk.
- **Beta** : Measures the correlation with the market or a benchmark. In many strategies, maintaining a low or negative beta might be preferred (for low-beta strategies like BAB).
