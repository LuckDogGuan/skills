## 讨论与评论 (2)

### 评论 #1 (作者: KP26017, 时间: 2个月前)

CSAP measures the collective performance of the alphas that have been selected into the live trading pool from your submissions. It's not a simple average of your individual alpha metrics — it's a portfolio-level performance measure that reflects how your selected alphas perform together as a combined set of positions in real market conditions.

The key word is selected — not all submitted alphas get selected into the pool. The platform evaluates submissions against quality thresholds, correlation constraints, and pool contribution criteria before selecting them. CSAP only counts performance from alphas that cleared this selection process and are actually contributing to live trading.
