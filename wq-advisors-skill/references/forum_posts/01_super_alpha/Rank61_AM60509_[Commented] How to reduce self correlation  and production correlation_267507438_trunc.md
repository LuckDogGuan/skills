- **Self-correlation (Autocorrelation)**
  - Use  **decorrelation (“signal whitening”)** —i.e., apply a filter or transformation to make the signal resemble white noise and thus reduce autocorrelation.
- **Production Correlation (Cross-correlation)**
  - Employ  **PCA**  (Principal Component Analysis) to convert correlated alphas into orthogonal (uncorrelated) components.
  - Apply  **Hierarchical Risk Parity (HRP)** : cluster similar signals and allocate weights based on risk, ensuring more diversified and decorrelated portfolios.
