## 帖子正文

This paper introduces "AlphaEvolve," a novel framework designed to discover new alpha factors by modeling scalar, vector, and matrix features. By leveraging AutoML techniques and selectively injecting relational domain knowledge, AlphaEvolve aims to enhance the predictability and diversity of alpha factors. The framework has been tested on real-world stock market data, demonstrating its effectiveness in improving stock trend forecasting and achieving higher returns in quantitative investment.

**Authors** : Can Cui, Wei Wang, Meihui Zhang, Gang Chen, Zhaojing Luo, Beng Chin Ooi
 **Link** :  [arXiv:2103.16196](https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.16196)

I'm excited to share a groundbreaking research paper that explores innovative methods for alpha generation in the global quantitative finance arena:

This study presents "AlphaEvolve," a framework that utilizes advanced machine learning techniques to uncover new alpha factors by modeling various feature types. The approach has shown promising results in enhancing stock trend forecasting and achieving superior returns.
