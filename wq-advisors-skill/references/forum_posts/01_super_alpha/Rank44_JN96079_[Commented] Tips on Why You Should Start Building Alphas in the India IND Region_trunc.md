## 帖子正文

I have been researching on IND region and i encourage you to dive into the IND region during alpha building because, One major advantage is its relationship with the ASI universes. Many ASI data fields also appear in IND, but the stocks themselves do not overlap. This creates an excellent opportunity:

- **Alphas that underperform in ASI can perform strongly in IND**
- **Alphas that succeed in both regions often become high-quality candidates**
- **Re-running or adapting ASI ideas in IND can lead to fast wins**

The region also offers generous trading flexibility—Max Trade is optional and turnover can go up to 40%. However, builders consistently see better results when keeping turnover under 20% and ensuring a smooth, investable PnL.

What makes IND even more compelling is its  **clear performance-driven theme** , which prioritizes robust, stable signals. Because of this, IND often provides  **higher payment potential** , especially for Alphas that show strong generalization and clean trading behavior.

### ⭐ Key Reasons to Try IND

- 500 highly liquid stocks with consistent behavior
- Extra Sharpe filter encourages more stable solutions
- Strong synergy with ASI data
- Flexible trade/turnover rules
- Higher earnings potential due to IND’s emphasis on robustness
- Great region for experimentation and transferring existing ideas

So together lets go India region
