### 评论 #2 (作者: NT84064, 时间: 1年前)

The key difference lies in the level of abstraction and composition capability:

🔹 Base-tagged operators are the fundamental building blocks — simple mathematical or statistical functions like  `rank` ,  `ts_mean` ,  `decay_linear` , etc. These operate directly on raw data or time series and are typically used to construct custom expressions manually.

🔹 Genius-tagged operators, on the other hand, are higher-level abstractions — often pre-engineered combinations of multiple base operators and logic that are designed to capture specific patterns or alpha hypotheses more effectively. They tend to be more expressive, sometimes integrating complex internal pipelines (e.g., denoising, normalization, or cross-sectional logic).

You can think of Genius operators as "macro tools", built using several "micro tools" from the Base set.
