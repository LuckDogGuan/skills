**🔹 Theme Overview** 
“All Regions / D1 Power Pool Apr’26” focuses on standardized Power Pool alphas.

**🔹 Duration** 
Runs from  **30 Mar’26 to 12 Apr’26 (2 weeks)** .

**🔹 Incentive** 
Provides  **1× multiplier**  for regular Power Pool alphas.

**🔹 Core Requirements**

- Delay =  **1 (D1)**
- Neutralization =  **RAM**

**🔹 Dataset Restrictions**

- **PV1 not allowed**  in KOR & IND (except support fields)
- **model110 not allowed**  in GLB region

**🔹 Objective** 
Promotes  **consistent, comparable alpha construction across regions** .

**🔹 Eligibility** 
Qualified alphas enter the  **Power Pool Thematic competition** .

### 评论 #3 (作者: KP26017, 时间: 2个月前)

RAM neutralization as the mandatory scheme is significant and worth understanding deeply before building. RAM removes exposures to well-documented risk factors — the residual that survives is genuinely idiosyncratic signal that can't be explained by known systematic premia. This is actually a quality filter in disguise — alphas that collapse under RAM were primarily capturing risk premia rather than genuine stock selection edge. Build expecting RAM neutralization from the start rather than developing on no neutralization and switching at submission.
