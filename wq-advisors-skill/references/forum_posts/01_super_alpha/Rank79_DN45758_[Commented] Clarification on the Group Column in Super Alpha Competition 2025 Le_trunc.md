### 评论 #3 (作者: TP18957, 时间: 1年前)

Hi everyone, just to add some clarity on the "Group" column — it essentially represents the weekly thematic category that the Super Alpha Competition (SAC) organizers set for participants. Each week, alphas submitted must align with that theme to be eligible for that group’s leaderboard ranking. This approach helps maintain fairness and relevance by grouping similar types of strategies or data focus areas together. It also encourages participants to diversify their research across different quantitative domains over time. If you don’t submit within the required category for that week, your alpha won’t count towards that group's standings. For detailed guidelines, check the official SAC support page linked by MA97359.
