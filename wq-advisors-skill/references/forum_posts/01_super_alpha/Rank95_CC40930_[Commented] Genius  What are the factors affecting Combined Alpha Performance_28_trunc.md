Regarding your question: "Is there any relation between Combined Alpha Performance and Combined Selected Alpha Performance?" As I understand it, "Combined Selected Alpha Performance" means that the system will randomly select alphas in your alpha pool to combine new alphas and calculate how effective these newly created alphas are. Therefore, if the alphas you initially submitted have good results, the results of "Combined Selected Alpha Performance" will usually be good.
