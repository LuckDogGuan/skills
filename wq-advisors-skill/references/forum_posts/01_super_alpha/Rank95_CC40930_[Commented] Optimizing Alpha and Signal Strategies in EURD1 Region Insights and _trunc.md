#### **Introduction**

After completing a Genius quarter, many consultants have likely determined their rank. However, with most consultants ranking from Expert and below, questions arise regarding how to efficiently exploit the EURD1 region and discover Alpha and Signal opportunities in this area. This article provides my personal perspective and suggestions on working with and analyzing the EURD1 region.

#### **1. Understanding EURD1, mdl230, and mdl109**

**EURD1 Region:**

- Focuses on European equities with a delay of 1 (D1), meaning signals are executed with a one-day lag.
- Offers a balance between high liquidity and moderate volatility, differing from regions like CHN (low liquidity, high volatility) or USA (high liquidity, strong volatility).
- The even liquidity distribution makes EURD1 an ideal environment for alpha strategies based on quantitative data.

**mdl230 – Liquidity Data:**

- Includes metrics such as bid-ask spread, order book imbalance, and market depth.
- Alpha creators use mdl230 to:
  - Identify low-liquidity stocks prone to significant price movements.
  - Leverage momentum or reversion models arising from liquidity imbalances.

**mdl109 – Fundamental Strength:**

- Comprises financial metrics such as revenue growth, earnings revisions, and profitability.
- This dataset is useful for building alphas with strong fundamental foundations, blending quantitative and qualitative factors.

#### **2. Approaches to Using mdl230**

**Exploiting Low Liquidity:**

- Low-liquidity stocks often exhibit price imbalances during market events.
- Alpha creators utilize mdl230 signals to identify such stocks and apply strategies such as:
  - **Short-term momentum:**  Price surges or drops driven by low liquidity.
  - **Reversion:**  Price corrections back to equilibrium after unexpected events.

**Combining with mdl109:**

- mdl230 detects stocks sensitive to capital flows, while mdl109 eliminates noise from financially unstable companies.
- Example: Focus on stocks with stable profit growth (mdl109) experiencing order book imbalances (mdl230).

#### **3. Approaches to Using mdl109**

**Financial Metric-Based Signals:**

- Alpha creators often seek signals from:
  - **Revenue growth:**  Predicts long-term price trends based on business strength.
  - **Earnings revisions:**  Stocks with upward profit revisions often show positive price trends.
  - **Profitability:**  Filters out weak stocks to avoid noise.

**Combining with mdl230:**

- When market liquidity is high (mdl230), signals from mdl109 tend to respond more quickly to fundamental changes.
- Conversely, during low liquidity phases, mdl109 signals can help identify long-term investment opportunities.
