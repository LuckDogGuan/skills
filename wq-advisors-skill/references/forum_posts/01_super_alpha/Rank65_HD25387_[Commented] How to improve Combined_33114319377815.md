## 帖子正文

Hello everyone, I’ve observed that the monthly Combined score is closely linked to the quality of the submitted alphas. However, to effectively improve this score, it’s not only important to ensure high-quality alphas, but also to meet a minimum quantity and maintain diversity — particularly in terms of Region and Neutralization. Therefore, I’m wondering how many high-quality alphas should ideally be submitted each month, with sufficient coverage, to truly make a positive impact on the Combined score. I would greatly appreciate hearing your thoughts on this.
