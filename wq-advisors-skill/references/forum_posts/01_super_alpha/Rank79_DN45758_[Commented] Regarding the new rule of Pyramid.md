### 评论 #2 (作者: 顾问 TN48242 (Rank 82), 时间: 1年前)

Hi PT88153, happy to help clarify! Starting from Q2 2025, each Alpha can now contribute to a maximum of 2 Genius pyramids only. If an Alpha uses more than 2 pyramids, it will still be valid for base payment and other Genius metrics (like #signals, CAP, tie-breakers), but won’t count further towards Genius pyramid requirements. This change encourages diversity in alpha construction and reduces the overuse of complex pyramid structures.

Additionally, pyramids using only neutralization fields (such as currency, country, exchange, sector, etc.) do not count as additional pyramids anymore. That means you can apply neutralization without it affecting your pyramid count.

The threshold for Genius titles has also been relaxed slightly: the requirement has changed from 10-30-60 pyramids to 10-20-50 for Expert, Master, and Grandmaster levels respectively.

Hope this helps! It's a good time to review your recent submissions to ensure they align with the updated rules.

### 评论 #11 (作者: DJ40095, 时间: 1年前)

Starting Q2 2025, WorldQuant has updated the rules for how  **pyramids**  contribute to Genius levels.

A  **pyramid**  is defined as a unique combination of  **region** ,  **delay** , and  **dataset category**  (e.g.,  `USA-D1-analyst` ). An Alpha can belong to more than one pyramid if it uses multiple data fields, but under the  **new rule** , each Alpha will count toward  **a maximum of 2 Genius pyramids** .

If an Alpha belongs to  **more than 2 pyramids** , it will contribute to  **zero Genius pyramids** , though it still counts for  **base payments**  and other Genius metrics like  **number of signals** ,  **Combined Alpha performance** , and  **tie-breakers** .

Also,  **neutralization fields**  (like country, sector, industry, etc.) do  **not**  count as additional pyramids. So, for example, if your Alpha uses 2 pyramids plus neutralization fields, it still counts fully toward Genius.

To complete a pyramid, you need to submit  **at least 3 Alphas**  within that same pyramid. Formulating multiple distinct pyramids is also encouraged, as it can help improve your  **Value Factor**  and  **Combined Alpha performance**  through diversification.

Here's a clear summary of the  **new Pyramid Submission Criteria**  in the  **WorldQuant Brain Genius Program**  (including SAC 2025):

- **Each Alpha can use data from a maximum of  **2 datasets/categories** .

  - **Price/Volume**

  - **Estimates**

- **Each Alpha can count toward only 2 pyramids maximum.**
- Even if it's high-quality, once used in 2 pyramids, it can’t be reused in another pyramid structure.

- This  **streamlines pyramid creation**  and keeps your designs compliant.

### 📌  **4. Reduced Pyramid Quotas**

- The total number of pyramids required per  **Genius level**  has been  **reduced** .
- This reflects a shift in emphasis from quantity to  **quality and efficiency** .

### 📌  **Design Tips:**

- ✅  **Plan your alpha set around a category**  — e.g., one week focus only on fundamental-based alphas.
- ✅ Use  **data profiling**  tools to ensure you don't exceed category limits.
- ✅ Keep expressions clean — avoid overly complex alphas with mixed category fields unless needed for fitness (but note: they won't count for pyramids).
