## 帖子正文

Combined Alpha Performance has been refreshed on the Genius leaderboard. This considers all the Alphas and SuperAlphas submitted during the entire OS period ending 30 Sept 2025.

[What is Combined Alpha Performance?](/hc/en-us/articles/26715994416535-What-is-Combined-Alpha-Performance)

- Combined out-sample performance of all consultant Alphas submitted by the consultant
- Combined out-sample performance of all consultant Alphas submitted by the consultant and then selected by WQ
- Combined out-sample performance of all Power Pool Alphas submitted by the consultant
- Higher value of the three as of the end of a quarter, would be used as a key criterion of the consultant levels in the BRAIN Genius program
- The metric would be refreshed every 4-6 weeks
