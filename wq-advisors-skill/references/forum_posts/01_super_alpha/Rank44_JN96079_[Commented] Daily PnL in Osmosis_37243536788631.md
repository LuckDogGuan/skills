### 评论 #11 (作者: NT84064, 时间: 6个月前)

The  **Daily PnL on the Osmosis leaderboard**  represents the mark-to-market performance of your current Osmosis allocation under  *live market conditions* , not a static backtest result. Even if you don’t change your selected alphas or osmosis points, Daily PnL will fluctuate because it reflects  **realized and unrealized returns driven by daily market movements** , factor interactions, and cross-alpha correlations. In other words, Osmosis is continuously exposed to changing volatility regimes, sector rotations, and covariance dynamics.

Its significance lies in  **short-horizon behavior diagnostics**  rather than long-term evaluation. Persistent negative Daily PnL may indicate crowding, correlation buildup, or regime sensitivity among your chosen alphas, while stable or improving Daily PnL suggests healthier diversification. However, it should not be overinterpreted in isolation—Daily PnL is noisy by nature. The real value is observing  *patterns over time* , especially during market stress, and comparing them against turnover, correlation drift, and alpha concentration. Used correctly, it’s a feedback signal for portfolio balance, not a performance verdict.
