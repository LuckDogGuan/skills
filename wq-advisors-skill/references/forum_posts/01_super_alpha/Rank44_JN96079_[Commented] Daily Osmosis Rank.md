### 评论 #17 (作者: LN21699, 时间: 3个月前)

Hi PM24512, totally agree with the rolling window approaches mentioned here. But with Super Alphas, the real game changer is managing Cost/Turnover Amplification and Orthogonality.

When you combine alphas, turnover often skyrockets. Allocating Osmosis points purely based on raw PnL or Sharpe is a trap. Instead, try rewarding regions that bring truly uncorrelated signals (orthogonality), and strictly penalize those that inflate your overall composite turnover.

Have you checked your regions using After-Cost IR or Sub-Universe Sharpe before allocating points? Often, a region with huge 'paper PnL' is exactly what's dragging your actual rank down due to hidden transaction costs.
