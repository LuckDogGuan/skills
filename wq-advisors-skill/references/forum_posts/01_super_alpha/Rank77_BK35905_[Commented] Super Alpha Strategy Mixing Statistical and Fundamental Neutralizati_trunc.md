## 帖子正文

### 评论 #2 (作者: 顾问 BK35905 (Rank 77), 时间: 8个月前)

Mixing neutralization can be effective — country-level removes broad macro and regional biases, while statistical neutralization cleans residual style or factor noise. Some creators find this combo produces more stable, diversified super alphas, though it can also over-sanitize if overdone. Best to test both approaches and compare robustness across universes.

Combining different forms of neutralization can be effective, with country-level neutralization stripping out broad macro and regional effects while statistical neutralization removes remaining style or factor noise. Some researchers find this mix leads to more stable and better-diversified SuperAlphas, though applying it too aggressively can over-sanitize the signal. It’s usually best to test both approaches and compare robustness across universes to find the right balance.
