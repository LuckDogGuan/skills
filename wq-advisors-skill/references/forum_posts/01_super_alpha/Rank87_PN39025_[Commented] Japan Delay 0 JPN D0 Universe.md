Exciting news—Japan’s Delay 0 universe is now available on Brain! With around 1,200 to 1,600 stocks, it’s not only the largest single-country universe in the ASI region but also one of the most liquid and important markets globally.

- What Is Different About JPN D0?
  Expectations must be adjusted because alpha returns are typically lower here than in other areas.
  Seasonality can be more useful than traditional momentum cues, which frequently fail.
  Newer data appears to be more important than earlier trends since post-COVID behavior is distinct.
  Although volume-based signals can still be effective, don't count on them to perform better overall.
  Advice for Using JPN D0:
  Pay attention to the stocks in the TOP1200, which are the most liquid.
  When possible, use local datasets; they have a significant impact.
  For a complete perspective, always compare the performance of your Alpha on both D0 and D1.
  Try value-based concepts if you're unsure where to begin; Model77 is a fantastic place to start.

Dig in, experiment, and don’t forget to share your findings!
