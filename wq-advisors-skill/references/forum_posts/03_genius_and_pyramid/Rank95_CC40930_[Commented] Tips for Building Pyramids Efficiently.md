## 帖子正文

When working on smaller regions, a good strategy is to start with data fields that have already generated signals in larger regions and are also available in other regions. For example, if "other428" performs well in the ASI region, you can also look for it in JPN. Similarly, you can leverage templates that have produced signals in other regions to explore commonly used datasets.

If your goal is to quickly increase the number of pyramids, prioritizing high-usage data fields is often more effective for generating alpha. However, keep in mind that this approach may lead to higher correlation with existing factors.

**Recommended Datasets:**

- **JPN** : other428, oth423
- **KOR** : model39, oth466
- **TWN** : model109

By following this approach, you can maximize efficiency while exploring opportunities to uncover new signals across different regions.
