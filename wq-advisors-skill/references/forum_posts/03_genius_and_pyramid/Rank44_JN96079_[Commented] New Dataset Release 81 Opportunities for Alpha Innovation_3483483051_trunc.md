## 帖子正文

With the release of  **81 new datasets**  spanning  **fundamentals, sentiment, news, short interest, macro, social media, and more** , we have a unique opportunity to push our research into fresh domains.

To make the most of this, I’d like to open a collaborative discussion around a few points:

### 🔎 1. Signal Viability

- Which datasets stand out as having  **regime-robust alpha potential**  rather than short-lived edge?
- Any experiences on  **mitigating IC decay**  when incorporating categories like short interest or social media?

### 🛠️ 2. Feature Engineering

- What  **transformations or operators**  (e.g., cross-sectional ranks, volatility-adjusted embeddings, regime-conditioned operators) have you found most effective for  **noisy datasets**  like news and sentiment?

### 📊 3. Integration with Existing Work

- How do you evaluate  **overlap / clustering risk**  when layering in new dataset-driven alphas?
- Any heuristics for  **early crowding detection**  during backtests?

### 🤝 4. Collaborative Knowledge Sharing

- If you’ve already explored any of these datasets, what  **conceptual approaches**  or  **pitfalls**  could be useful for others to know?

These datasets are an exciting addition, but the real edge will come from  **how quickly and effectively we distill them into production-viable signals** .

I’d encourage consultants who have begun experimenting to share perspectives, so we can accelerate past the trial-and-error stage as a community.
