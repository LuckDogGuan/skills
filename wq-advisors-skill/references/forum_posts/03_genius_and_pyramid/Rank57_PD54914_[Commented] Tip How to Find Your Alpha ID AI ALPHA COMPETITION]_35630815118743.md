# Tip: How to Find Your Alpha ID {AI ALPHA COMPETITION]

## 帖子正文

If you’ve just simulated an alpha and want to find its  **Alpha ID** , here’s how to do it:

1️⃣ Go to the  **Results**  section after simulating your alpha.
2️⃣  select  **“Open link in new tab.”** 
3️⃣ In the new tab, look at the  **URL**  in your browser’s address bar — the  **last set of letters**  after  `/alpha/`  is your  **Alpha ID** .

📍Example:
 `https://platform.worldquantbrain.com/alpha/ABC123XYZ`  → Your Alpha ID is  **ABC123XYZ**
