While analyzing the  **Community Score rankings** , I noticed something quite interesting. Among the  **top ~110 consultants**  ranked by Community Score, a significant majority appear to be from  **Mainland China** . I've also noticed a noticeable presence from  **South Korea, Taiwan, Singapore, and Malaysia.**

This made me wonder — is there any  **specific reason**  why these regions are so dominant in the Community Score leaderboard?
Are there  **regional factors, educational focus, different collaboration habits, or perhaps localized programs**  that encourage higher Community participation from these areas?
Or do these consultants receive any  **region-specific opportunities, incentives, or scoring adjustments**  that others might not be aware of?

I am asking purely from  **curiosity**  and with the intention to  **learn** . It's impressive to see this consistency, and understanding the reasons behind it could help others improve their own performance and approach.

If anyone has insights, would love to hear your thoughts.
Thanks!
