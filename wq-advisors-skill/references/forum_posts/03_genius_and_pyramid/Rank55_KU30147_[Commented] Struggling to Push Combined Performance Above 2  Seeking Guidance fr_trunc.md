### 评论 #2 (作者: KP26017, 时间: 2个月前)

Osmosis is particularly sensitive to  **uniqueness** . If your alpha correlates too highly with the existing WorldQuant alpha library, Osmosis score drops sharply. The fix here is:

- Use  **unusual operator combinations**  rather than standard textbook factors
- Combine operators in non-obvious sequences
- Avoid well-known factors like simple price momentum or basic P/E ratios in their raw form — transform them in novel ways
