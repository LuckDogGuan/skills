1️⃣  **Recognition:**  They reflect your contribution quality and consistency, which can be valuable for credibility in community discussions or collaborations.
2️⃣  **Access:**  Occasionally, higher levels are prioritized for pilot programs, beta tests, or internal research invitations.
3️⃣  **Motivation:**  They serve as milestones to track your growth as a quant researcher.

So while badges don’t directly change your account features, they definitely carry weight within the ecosystem — both in reputation and future opportunities.
