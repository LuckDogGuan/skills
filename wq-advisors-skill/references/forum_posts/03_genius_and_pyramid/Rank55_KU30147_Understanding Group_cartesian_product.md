## 帖子正文

group_cartesian_product creates all pairwise combinations of values within each group (e.g., sector, country). It’s used to model interactions between entities—such as stock–peer or factor–factor relationships—while preserving group boundaries, enabling relative comparison, interaction effects, or network-style signals without cross-group leakage.Proper format to use it.group_rank(x,group_cartesian_product(industry, subindustry))
