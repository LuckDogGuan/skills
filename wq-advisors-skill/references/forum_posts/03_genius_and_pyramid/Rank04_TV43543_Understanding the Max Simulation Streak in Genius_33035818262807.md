## 帖子正文

I want to gain a deeper understanding of the Max Simulation Streak metric in Genius. For example, by the 10th day of the first month of the quarter, my simulation streak is 200. However, I miss simulating for one day, so my simulation streak resets to 1. By the end of the quarter, my streak is 80. In this case, what would my Max Simulation Streak be at the end of the quarter 200 or 80 ? Thank you everyone.

### 评论 #3 (作者: SD92473, 时间: 1年前)

Great question! The  **Max Simulation Streak**  in Genius refers to the  **longest uninterrupted sequence of daily simulations**  within a quarter. In your example, you had a streak of 200 by the 10th day but missed a day, which reset your current streak to 1. Even though your new streak reached 80 by quarter-end, the earlier streak of 200 still stands as your  **maximum streak** —as it was the longest continuous period of simulation during the quarter. So, at the end of the quarter, your  **Max Simulation Streak would be 200** , not 80. This metric helps track consistency over time, and maintaining a high max streak signals strong and regular engagement with the platform.
