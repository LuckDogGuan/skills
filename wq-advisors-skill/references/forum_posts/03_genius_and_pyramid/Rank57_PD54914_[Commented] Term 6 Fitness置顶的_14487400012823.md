## 帖子正文

Fitness is defined in the Alpha Performance help page: Fitness = Sharpe * sqrt(abs(Returns) / Max(Turnover,0.125)).

Fitness is similar to ELO ratings in chess, which make it easier to compare two chess players.

### 评论 #11 (作者: 顾问 PD54914 (Rank 57), 时间: 1个月前)

Comparing Fitness to Elo ratings is a spot-on analogy for understanding its role in standardizing strategy evaluation! Just like an Elo rating balances a player's win rate against the difficulty of their matches, the Fitness formula beautifully balances a signal's risk-adjusted performance (Sharpe) and absolute profitability (Returns) against the frictional costs of trading (Turnover). It acts as a perfect equalizer, heavily penalizing alphas that trade too frequently for too little gain. This ensures that only the most cost-efficient and robust models rise to the top of the leaderboard, much like grandmasters in a tournament.
