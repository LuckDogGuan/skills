### 评论 #3 (作者: MB13430, 时间: 2年前)

answer is  **400 AU per month**

Let the first segment be S1 AU
And second segment is S2 AU

Months taken by alpha= S1/300 + S2/600

Months taken by beta= S1/600 + S2/300

Time taken by alpha = Time taken by beta

S1/300 + S2/600 = S1/600 + S2/300

2S1 + S2 = S1 + 2S2

S1 = S2

Let Gamma speed be Vgamma

Tgamma = (S1+S2)/Vgamma

Time taken by alpha = Time taken by gamma

S1/300 + S2/600 = (S1+S2)/Vgamma

2S1 + S2)/600 = (S1+S2)/Vgamma

3S1/600 = 2S1/Vgamma

Vgamma = 600*2/3

**Vgamma = 400 AU per month**

**For alpha spaceship Total time required to reach its Destination:**

D1/300+D2/600

**For Beta spaceship Total time required to reach its Destination:**

D1/600+D2/(300*λ)

Note : Speed of beta for 2nd segment is not more than 300 so,

# 0<λ<=1

**For gamma spaceship Total time required to reach its Destination:**

D1/v+D2/v
