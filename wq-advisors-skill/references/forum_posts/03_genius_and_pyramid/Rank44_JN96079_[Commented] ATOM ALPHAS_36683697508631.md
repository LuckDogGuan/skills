### 评论 #1 (作者: AK40989, 时间: 6个月前)

ATOM alphas can help your Genius CAP, but only indirectly. What actually improves CAP is consistent submission of high-quality signals with good stability, low production correlation, and solid Sharpe. If your ATOM alphas meet those criteria, they’ll contribute just like any other strong alpha.

Breaking Genius tiers mostly comes down to three things: keep turnover reasonable, avoid repeated structures, and maintain low prod-correlation across your whole set. Running diverse datasets and focusing on clean, simple operator chains tends to work better than pushing too much complexity.
