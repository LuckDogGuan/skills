### 评论 #5 (作者: LB76673, 时间: 9个月前)

The platform has been very clear about thresholds for Combined Alpha Performance (CAP) and Combined Selected Alpha Performance (CSAP), but for  **Combined Power Pool Alpha Performance (CPAP)**  there hasn’t been a published set of cutoffs yet. From what’s been mentioned in webinars, CPAP is being introduced as a complementary metric to encourage consultants to curate strong, low-correlation PowerPool submissions, but the eligibility bands for Genius levels haven’t been fixed or shared publicly. It’s likely that thresholds will not simply mirror CAP/CSAP because PowerPool dynamics (self-correlation, overlap, production stability) behave differently. Some hints suggest the platform may adjust criteria dynamically as more data accumulates, rather than locking it in immediately. For now, the safe approach is to treat CPAP as a qualitative indicator of how much value your pool adds, while CAP and CSAP remain the decisive gates for promotions.
