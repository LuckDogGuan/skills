DST involves shifting the clock  **forward by 1 hour in spring**  and  **back by 1 hour in fall** , which impacts reset times for users in other time zones, such as Vietnam (GMT+7).

### Recent and Upcoming DST Changes:

- **Last Change** : On  **Sunday, November 3, 2024** , the US transitioned  **out of DST** , moving clocks  **back by 1 hour** . This increased the time difference between Vietnam and the US by 1 hour, causing the alpha reset time to shift from  **11 AM to 12 PM (GMT+7)** .
- **Next Change** : On  **Sunday, March 9, 2025** , the US will transition  **into DST** , moving clocks  **forward by 1 hour** . At that time, the alpha reset time is expected to return to  **11 AM (GMT+7)** .

   - Fills missing or zero values in time series data using past observations.
   - Syntax:  `ts_backfill(x, d)`  replaces NaN or zeros in  `x`  with the most recent non-missing value from the past  `d`  days, ensuring continuity.
2. **Group Backfill (group_backfill)** :
   - Leverages group information for missing data.
   - Syntax:  `group_backfill(x, group, d)`  replaces missing values in  `x`  with the winsorized mean of other instruments in the same group over  `d`  days.
