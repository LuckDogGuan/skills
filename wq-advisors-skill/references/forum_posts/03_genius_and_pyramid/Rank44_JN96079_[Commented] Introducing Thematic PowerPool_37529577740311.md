## 帖子正文

The  **Thematic PowerPool**  brings a more  **focused and streamlined approach**  to PowerPool competitions, with clearly defined themes and reduced eligibility barriers.

🔑  **Key Changes:**

- Shorter, more focused  **thematic competitions**
- Centrally identified  **value-adding directions**
- Reduced eligibility:  **minimum 5 alphas**
- **Pure PowerPool alphas**  only (default multiplier 1x)

🔄  **What stays the same:**

- Existing  **PowerPool tagging methodology**
- Daily and monthly quotas
- Combined PowerPool score in Genius includes all tagged alphas

🗓  **January Competitions:**

- **IND:**  5–18 Jan | Robust Universe Sharpe > 1
- **GLB:**  19 Jan – 1 Feb | Select Datasets

🏆  **Awards:** 
Prizes for top  **40 performers** , with payouts starting from  **$600 (1st place)**  and extending across the leaderboard.

If you’re looking to align research with  **high-impact themes**  and maximize PowerPool effectiveness, this is a great opportunity 🚀
