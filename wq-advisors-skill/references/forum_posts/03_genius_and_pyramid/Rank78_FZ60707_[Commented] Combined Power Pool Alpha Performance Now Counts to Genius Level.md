## 帖子正文

The Genius Status page now includes a new performance metric: Combined Power Pool Alpha Performance. This is the latest addition to the alpha performance criteria and reflects the combined performance of all your Power Pool Alphas tagged as of 31-05-2025.

As per the updated rules, your Genius level will be based on the maximum of these three:

- Combined Alpha Performance
- Combined Selected Alpha Performance
- Combined Power Pool Alpha Performance ← (newly added)

Final Genius levels will be announced by 9 July 2025.

I’m curious to hear from others how did your Power Pool combined score turn out? Was it in line with your expectations? Did it make a difference in helping you qualify for the Q2 Genius Level? Let’s compare notes, this could be useful for those trying to understand how to prioritize Power Pool tagging next quarter.

### 评论 #5 (作者: NT84064, 时间: 10个月前)

The inclusion of Combined Power Pool Alpha Performance as an additional metric for Genius level evaluation is a notable shift because it changes the optimization problem for consultants. Previously, one could focus primarily on maximizing Combined Alpha Performance or Combined Selected Alpha Performance, but now Power Pool composition directly affects the outcome. Since the Genius level is determined by the maximum of the three metrics, a strong Power Pool score can compensate for weaker performance in the other two — particularly for those whose best alphas happen to be Power Pool tagged. This reinforces the importance of carefully curating which alphas are tagged, ensuring they’re both high-quality and synergistic to maintain strong combined performance. It also suggests that quarterly tagging decisions should be made with as much rigor as alpha construction itself, especially if certain universes or turnover profiles are performing better in the Power Pool environment.
