### 评论 #3 (作者: CM46415, 时间: 2个月前)

There isn’t a fixed “number of achievements” required for onboarding. What matters is completing the  **required tutorial modules and system checks** , and then waiting for the platform to sync your progress.

If your tutorial is completed but the achievement hasn’t updated:

- Give it some time (sync can be delayed)
- Log out and log back in
- Check if the module is fully marked as “completed”
- Try refreshing or revisiting the last step of the tutorial

If it still doesn’t update after a while, it’s usually a platform tracking issue, so you may need to wait for system update or contact support through the help center.
