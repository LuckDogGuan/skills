## 讨论与评论 (3)

### 评论 #1 (作者: 顾问 PN39025 (Rank 87), 时间: 2个月前)

The osmosis score is currently calculated based on the number of days it takes for all your alpha to generate profit or loss, but it has just been updated to be calculated over 250 days. Rest assured, high or low weight will not affect your osmosis score. Good luck!

---

### 评论 #2 (作者: CM46415, 时间: 2个月前)

No. Osmosis points are  **not affected by weight factor** .

Weight mainly affects how strongly an alpha contributes inside a portfolio or combined signal, but the  **Osmosis score is computed independently**  based on performance over the evaluation window (now ~250 days).

So even without weight:

- Your alphas can still earn osmosis points
- Performance is still fully reflected
- Only actual alpha behavior (PnL over time) matters, not weighting

---

### 评论 #3 (作者: KP26017, 时间: 2个月前)

Osmosis points and weight factor are not the same thing and one doesn't strictly require the other to function — but they are connected through the underlying alpha quality that drives both.
