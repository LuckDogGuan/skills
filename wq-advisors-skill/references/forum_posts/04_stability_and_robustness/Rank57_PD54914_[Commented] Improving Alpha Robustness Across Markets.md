## 帖子正文

To best achieve alpha resilience, test across market regimes and instruments. 
Apply metrics like Sharpe ratio, drawdown stability, and correlation decay across geographies and horizons. 
Apply walk-forward optimization and out-of-sample testing to ensure robustness. 
Reducing volatility usually arises from normalizing exposure, averaging uncorrelated alphas, and avoiding overfitting through feature regularization.
