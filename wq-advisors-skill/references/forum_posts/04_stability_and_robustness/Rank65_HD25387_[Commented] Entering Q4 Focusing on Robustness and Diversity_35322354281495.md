## 帖子正文

Hi everyone,

As Q4 begins, I’ve been reflecting on lessons from the past cycle. A clear takeaway for me was how much  *robustness vs. overfitting*  matters. Some expressions looked excellent in-sample but lost effectiveness once OS started. In contrast, the ones that held up tended to share two traits:

- **Dataset diversity**  — blending sentiment or analyst inputs with fundamental signals proved more resilient than relying on a single data source.
- **Moderation in transformations**  — avoiding excessive chaining of operators often preserved interpretability and stability.

For this quarter, I plan to focus less on “exotic complexity” and more on building signals that hold up across universes and regions. I’ll also be keeping a closer eye on portfolio weight concentration, since a few dominant names distorted my results last time.

I’d be very interested to hear how others here are approaching Q4. Are you making specific adjustments to improve OS stability, or are you leaning toward doubling down on innovation?
