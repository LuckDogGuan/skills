**论文题目** ：Are Cash Flows Better Stock Return Predictors than Profits?
 **链接** ： [https://ssrn.com/abstract=2472571](https://ssrn.com/abstract=2472571)

**Author** : Stephen R. Foerster, John Tsagarelis, Grant Wang

**Year** : 2017

这实际是一篇平台里的research paper for users,链接为 [https://support.worldquantbrain.com/hc/en-us/community/posts/14932488666903-Research-paper-04-Are-Cash-Flows-Better-Stock-Return-Predictors-than-Profits-](https://support.worldquantbrain.com/hc/en-us/community/posts/14932488666903-Research-paper-04-Are-Cash-Flows-Better-Stock-Return-Predictors-than-Profits-)

**核心思想** ：论文中利用营运现金流减去资本支出的各种估计构建盈利指标，我的alpha也是基于此，并结合企业价值与毛利率做了适当调整。

**相关数据集:**

cashflow_op

capex

enterprise_value

mdf_gpr

**主要代码**

# Calculate cash flow from operations (CFO) adjusted for capital expenditures (CAPEX)

cfo_adj = subtract(cashflow_op, capex);

# Calculate cash flow to enterprise value ratio using the adjusted CFO

cashflow_to_ev_adj = divide(cfo_adj, enterprise_value);

cashflow_rank_adj = rank(cashflow_to_ev_adj);

# Calculate the growth in gross profit as before but include an adjustment for CAPEX growth;

gpr_growth_adj = ts_delta(mdf_gpr, 30);

capex_growth = ts_delta(capex, 30);

gpr_growth_cfo_adj = subtract(gpr_growth_adj, capex_growth);

gpr_rank_adj = rank(gpr_growth_cfo_adj);

# Combine the ranks of the adjusted cash flow to EV ratio and the adjusted GPR growth

combined_alpha_adj = multiply(cashflow_rank_adj, gpr_rank_adj);

rank(combined_alpha_adj);
