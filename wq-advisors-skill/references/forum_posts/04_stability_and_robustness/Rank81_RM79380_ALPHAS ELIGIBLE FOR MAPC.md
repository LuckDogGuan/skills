### 评论 #7 (作者: AS77213, 时间: 9个月前)

All Global D1 (daily frequency) risk-neutralized alphas that successfully meet standard performance evaluation criteria—such as strong returns, low drawdowns, and acceptable risk-adjusted metrics—qualify for inclusion in the MAPC pipeline. Risk-neutralization ensures that alphas are not unintentionally biased toward common risk factors, improving robustness. Passing these tests indicates the alpha is both statistically sound and practically viable, making it a candidate for further deployment or portfolio integration.
