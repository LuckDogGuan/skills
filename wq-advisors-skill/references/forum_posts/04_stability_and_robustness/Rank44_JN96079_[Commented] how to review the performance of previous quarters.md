### 评论 #4 (作者: TP19085, 时间: 5个月前)

At present, access to detailed historical quarter views is fairly limited. You can change the selected quarter using the dashboard dropdown to review eligibility status and applicable tie-breakers for that specific period, but there is no comprehensive archive that provides a full breakdown or side-by-side time-series comparison across quarters. As a result, it’s not yet possible to easily track how metrics or qualification status evolve over longer horizons directly within the platform. For researchers who want to maintain longitudinal records, the most practical workaround is to manually export key metrics or save screenshots at the end of each quarter. While this approach is not ideal, it does allow you to build your own historical record and compare progress over time until more robust archival or analytics features are introduced.
