The  **Merged Alpha Performance Competition (MAPC 2025)**  goes live on  **September 8th** !
Get ready to  **merge your best Global Region Risk-Neutralized Alphas**  and compete against fellow consultants for a  **$30,000 total stipend pool!**

This is our chance to  **prove our skills, push our limits, and rise as a top BRAIN Consultant.**

### 🏆  **Stipend Rewards**

- 🥇 1st Place –  **$3,000**
- 🥈 2nd Place –  **$2,000**
- 🥉 3rd Place –  **$1,000**
- 4th–10th –  **$700**
- 11th–25th –  **$500**
- 26th–50th –  **$300**
- 🌍  **Top 3 from each country**  (outside top 50) –  **$100**
  *(Min 10 alphas required)*

### 💡  **How the MAPC Score is Calculated**

Our score is based on the  **merged performance series** , using  **equal dollar-weighted investment**  in each Alpha.

**Key Metrics:**

- **Sharpe Ratio**  – Higher Sharpe = higher score 📈
- **Returns to Drawdown Ratio**  – Strong, stable returns win

**Weighting:**

- **25% IS Period**
- **75% OS Period**

🔹  *Consistency and OS strength matter most!*
