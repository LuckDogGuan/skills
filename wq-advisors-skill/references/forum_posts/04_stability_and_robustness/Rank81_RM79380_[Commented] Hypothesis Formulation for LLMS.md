### 评论 #1 (作者: DO92149, 时间: 4个月前)

Formulating good hypothesis can be challenging but a starting point can be making sure to avoid:

- too many conditions
- nested logic
- “if-then” conditions
- exact numeric windows

### 评论 #4 (作者: EO45950, 时间: 3个月前)

I always start with simple, testable hypotheses grounded in economic intuition. Avoid overfitting by limiting parameters. Combine LLM insights with traditional factors, validate across regimes, and focus on robustness, not just Sharpe or backtest performance.
