## 帖子正文

There’s always a trade-off between simple, interpretable signals and more complex constructions that might squeeze out extra alpha. Simple signals are usually more robust, but sometimes they underperform in raw returns. How do you decide when to stop adding layers like ranks, neutralization, or nonlinear transforms? Do you favor stability over marginal improvements, or chase performance even if interpretability drops?
