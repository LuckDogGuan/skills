## 帖子正文

It all comes down to a simple formula:IR = Return / VolatilityThe objective is straightforward: improve the ratio by either increasing returns or reducing volatility.To enhance returns, focus on improving predictive power. This can be achieved by combining multiple data sources—such as price and volume data for shorter-term signals, or fundamentals and analyst estimates for longer-term opportunities. In many cases, simpler models are more robust and easier to maintain.To lower volatility, identify the main sources of noise in the signal. Broad market movements, sector biases, or highly unstable stocks can all increase risk. Techniques such as neutralization can help reduce unwanted exposures and produce more consistent performance.There is no universal formula for success. Progress comes from continuous experimentation, learning from the research community, and steadily refining your ideas over time.
