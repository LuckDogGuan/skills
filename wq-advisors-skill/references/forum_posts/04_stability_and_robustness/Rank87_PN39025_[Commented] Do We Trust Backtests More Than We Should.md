### 评论 #3 (作者: CM46415, 时间: 2个月前)

Yes;sometimes we  *over-trust*  smooth backtests.

A smooth equity curve can be real edge, but it can also come from overfitting, hidden lag, or low-frequency noise that won’t survive live trading. The key is to verify robustness: sub-universes, different regimes, and out-of-sample stability.
