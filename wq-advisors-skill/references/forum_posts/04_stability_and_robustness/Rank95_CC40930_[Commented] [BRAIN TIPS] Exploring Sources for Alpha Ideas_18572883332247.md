## 帖子正文

Alpha ideas can be found online via research papers, finance journals and technical indicators.

- Notable research papers and finance journals:  [SSRN](http://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/JELJOUR_Results.cfm?form_name=journalBrowse&journal_id=179252) ,  [Seeking Alpha](http://seekingalpha.com/)  and  [Wilmott](http://www.wilmott.com/)
- Notable blogs:  [Epchan](http://epchan.blogspot.sg/)  and  [AuTraSy](http://autrasy.wpengine.netdna-cdn.com/)
- Technical Indicators: Useful for analyzing short-term price movements. They seek to predict future price levels or general price direction based on past patterns.

o   Common Indicators: Relative Strength Index, Money Flow Index, Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), Bollinger Bands, etc.

o   Reference website:  [Stock Charts](https://stockcharts.com/school/doku.php?id=chart_school)  and  [Incredible Charts](https://www.incrediblecharts.com/) .

More Concepts to Explore:

- Price movement and technical indicators
- Various measures of volatility: historical, implied, volatility index, intraday, etc.
- Interactions between volume and price. For example, is volume positively correlated with absolute price changes?
- Identification of short and long-term market  [trends](http://www.investopedia.com/articles/technical/03/060303.asp)
