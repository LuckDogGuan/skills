## 帖子正文

Now that Q1 2026 is already underway, this is a good moment to realign priorities on WorldQuant BRAIN and focus on steady, long-term progress rather than short-term wins. Strong performance comes from continuous experimentation, learning from failures, and consistently refining ideas.

Key focus areas for Q1 2026:

- Build  **new alpha ideas**  instead of over-optimizing existing ones
- Submit alphas  **consistently** , even if they’re not perfect
- Keep logic  **simple, clean, and economically intuitive**
- Actively work on  **lowering production and self-correlation**
- Improve  **robustness, turnover control, and correlation management**
- Submit more  **theme-based alphas**
- Increase focus on  **single-dataset alphas**  for cleaner signals
- Track  **learning and progress** , not just submission scores

Q1 2026 is about discipline, creativity, and persistence. Small improvements compound over time—stay curious, stay consistent, and keep pushing forward.
