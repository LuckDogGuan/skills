# KEY METRICS AND TIPS TO CONSIDER BEFORE SUBMITTING AN ALPHA

## 帖子正文

1. **Constrain Turnover**  – Ensure that turnover remains below 25%, thereby preserving stability while minimizing excessive position reshuffling.
2. **Maintain Adequate Margin**  – Strive for margins consistently above 5%, reinforcing both resilience and profitability of the signal.
3. **Optimize Risk-Adjusted Performance**  – Prioritize higher return-to-drawdown ratios, reflecting efficiency in capital utilization under stress.
4. **Sharpe Ratio Excellence**  – Target Sharpe ratios that not only exceed baseline thresholds but also outperform peer benchmarks.
5. **Elevated Fitness Levels**  – Pursue fitness scores well above average to demonstrate robustness across multiple evaluation horizons.
6. **Mitigate Correlation Overlap**  – Avoid submitting alphas with high mutual correlation to ensure true diversification of predictive power.
7. **Suppress Noisy Constructs**  – Refrain from deploying alphas that exhibit excessive stochastic noise, focusing instead on structural, repeatable patterns.
8. **Consistency Across Horizons**  – Confirm that TRAIN, TEST, and IS phases all exhibit positive performance metrics, thereby validating generalizability and reducing overfitting risk.
