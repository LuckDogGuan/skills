## 帖子正文

I’m unable to generate alpha in the CHN region due to low Robust Universe Sharpe and low IS Ladder Sharpe. Could you please suggest ways to improve it?

---

## 讨论与评论 (4)

### 评论 #1 (作者: SK13215, 时间: 8个月前)

1. **Generate new CHN alphas**  with diverse ideas (liquidity, volatility, sentiment, etc.).
2. **Test across all CHN universes**  to identify stable performers.
3. **Filter out**  alphas with high correlation or universe instability.
4. **Refine and combine**  complementary ones to boost RUS.
5. **Monitor over time**  — if Sharpe decays quickly, reduce parameter sensitivity.
