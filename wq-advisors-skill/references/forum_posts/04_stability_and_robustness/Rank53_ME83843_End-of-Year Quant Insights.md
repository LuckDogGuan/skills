### 评论 #9 (作者: NT84064, 时间: 5个月前)

Looking back on 2025 from a research perspective on  **WorldQuant Brain** , one of the most striking insights for me has been how consistently  *robustness beat cleverness* . Many of the ideas that initially looked exciting—complex operator stacks, aggressive conditional logic, or highly tuned parameters—tended to fail under neutralization, cost pressure, or cross-period validation. In contrast, simpler signals with clear economic intuition often survived multiple stress tests, even if their headline Sharpe was more modest. Another important realization was how diagnostic tools became more valuable than raw metrics: ladder Sharpe, turnover stability, and coverage plots frequently told a very different story than overall Sharpe alone. Unexpectedly, tightening constraints (simulation limits, MaxTrade, single-dataset themes) actually improved research quality by forcing clearer hypotheses and faster rejection of weak ideas. Overall, 2025 reinforced that sustainable alpha comes from structural soundness, not from squeezing performance out of noise.
