## 帖子正文

**标题** ： [Profitability, R&D Investments and the Cross-Section of Stock Returns](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3178186)

**Abstract** ：In this paper, after controlling for the level of R&D expenditures, I find that profitability of R&D intensive firms is more important for subsequent returns than the R&D intensity (measured with R&D-to-market value or R&D-to-assets) and past performance. In a sample of firms where I am able to compute abnormal returns around quarterly earnings announcements R&D-to-market value variable becomes insignificant even when not controlling for the firm’s profitability. Since quarterly profits are reported by big firms this suggests that R&D anomaly is driven by small stocks. Big firms with high R&D-to-market value earn lower not higher subsequent returns, once I control for the level of R&D expenditures and firm’s profitability. The previously recorded anomaly, namely that R&D intensive firms earn positive abnormal returns, seems to be driven by the level of R&D investment, its profitability and small stocks.

**Alpha Idea**

控制住R&D-to-profits和cap，做多profitability高的股票，做空profitability低的股票。

主要的依据是文中两处

> Profitability subsumes the power of R&D-to-market value (RDM) to predict future returns once I control for R&D-to-profits and past performance.

> The previously recorded anomaly, namely that R&D intensive firms earn positive abnormal returns, seems to be driven by the level of R&D investment, its profitability and small stocks.

**数据**

- Dataset: Company Fundamental Data for Equity

`rd_expense`  ：Research And Development (Quarterly)

coverage=57%，除个别离群点全在[-0.01, 0.01]，正负各一半，季度更新

`fnd6_newqv1300_xrdq` ：Research and Development Expense

coverage=44%, 全是正的，季度更新

`revenue` : revenue-total

coverage=97%, 季度更新

`cogs` ：Cost of Goods Sold

coverage=97%，季度更新

`assets` ：Assets - Total

coverage=100%，季度更新

`bookvalue_ps` ：Book Value Per Share

coverage=98%，年度更新

- Dataset: Price Volume Data for Equity

`sharesout` : Daily outstanding shares (in millions)，日更，但是变化可能不大

`cap` ：Daily market capitalization (in millions)，日更

**Alpha Settings**

Universe：USA1000（文中去掉金融公司，assets<12million或bookequity<12.5million的小公司，以及算不出数据的公司，最终参与计算的每次平均920个公司）

Neutralization：Subindustry（未提及，考虑不跨行业比较研发投入，暂定subindustry）*最终设定里改为Market

Decay: 0

Delay: 1

Truncation: 0.08

Pasteurization: On (Not specified in the paper)

NaN Handling: Off (Not specified in the paper)

Unit Handling: Verify (Not specified in the paper)

**V1.1**

用revenue-cogs计算gross_profit，简单测试gross_profit，几乎都是正的，分布看上去比较合理，可以认为是gross-profit的绝对值。用rd_expense表示研发投入。根据文中的说法，用rd-to-gross profit（RDGP）代表投资水平，gross profit-to-assets（GPA）表示profitability。主要因子是rank(GPA)。

此时遇到第一个问题，数据rd_expense似乎并不是研发投入的绝对值，而是正负各一半，绝对值小于0.01的数字。为了避开用绝对值，我用rank(ts_delta(RDM, 60))表示公司加大了研发投入。也就是说研发投入越高且盈利能力越高的公司越被做多。
