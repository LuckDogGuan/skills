Greetings!

Together with the MAPC Star start, here are the exciting new features for you to explore:

- **GLB Region Submissions for MAPC Star** . Check out the newly added "Region" in Settings.
- **Daily Quota** . You can now submit up to four Alphas per day. Choose wisely!
- **Visualization.**  This feature, which allows you to view the simulated coverage, PnL, Size, and Sharpe of your Alphas across sectors, industries, and countries (for GLB region), can help you identify concentration bias and improve it.
- **Multi-Simulation.**  Save time by simulating multiple Alphas simultaneously. Up to 10 Alphas with the same region and delay can be simulated in one go, and three multi-simulations can run concurrently.
- **Prod-Correlation.**  Your Alpha correlations will be tested not only against your own Alphas but also against all the existing Alphas in the whole consultant pool.
- **Extended IS Period** . The In-Sample period has been extended from 5 to 10 years, offering a more extensive view of your Alphas' performance. You can voluntarily set aside 2 years as an evaluation period for yourself (Test Period).
- **Performance comparison for MAPC Star.**  This feature now includes all Global Region Alphas submitted since the start of MAPC Star.
