## 帖子正文

- **Base your PowerPool Alphas on sound economic logic.**
  Ensure each Alpha reflects a rational and data-supported market behavior rather than random statistical patterns.
- **Avoid mining PowerPool Alphas using the API.**
  Focus on meaningful research and idea generation rather than brute-force Alpha creation.
- **Submit only Alphas you truly understand.**
  Even though submission criteria are flexible, every Alpha in your PowerPool should have a clear concept and purpose.
- **Tag your PowerPool Alphas properly for diversity.**
  Don’t overload your PowerPool with similar Alphas based on the same dataset or underlying logic.
- **Leverage PowerPool for improved combined performance.**
  PowerPool can significantly enhance your overall results by adding  *diversity*  to your Alpha portfolio—use this feature wisely!
