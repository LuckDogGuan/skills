### 评论 #6 (作者: AY28568, 时间: 6个月前)

Universe choice has a huge impact on alpha behavior because liquidity, coverage, and factor exposures change materially across universes. I usually start with a broader universe (like TOP3000) to test robustness, then validate on tighter universes to check stability. If an alpha only works in one universe, it’s often overfitted to microstructure effects. Designing with liquidity filters, neutralization, and cross-region tests helps build signals that generalize better rather than collapse when the universe changes.
