## 帖子正文

**Compensation:** 
Consultants receive periodic base payments and quarterly rewards based on alpha quality. They may also earn referral fees and be considered for internships, full-time roles, and exclusive Brain networking events.

**Simulation Insights:** 
New visualization tools show coverage, PnL, size, and Sharpe across sectors/industries to detect concentration bias and improve alphas.

**Expanded Access:** 
Consultants gain access to European, Chinese, and Asian markets plus exclusive datasets. New datasets can help develop or adapt alpha ideas, but overfitting should be avoided.
