## 帖子正文

NaN stands for ‘Not a Number‘. It is used to indicate results of invalid operations like division by zero or if some data is unavailable.

Consider a chess player who has won 10 games and lost zero. His win-loss ratio would be 10/0 games. Since division by 0 is not valid, the ratio would have a NaN value.
