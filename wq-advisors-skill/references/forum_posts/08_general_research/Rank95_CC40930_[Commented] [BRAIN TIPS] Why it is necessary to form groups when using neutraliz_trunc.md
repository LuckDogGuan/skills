# [BRAIN TIPS] Why it is necessary to form groups when using neutralization?

## 帖子正文

Neutralization reduces the risk of making a directional bet by allotting the same amount of dollars long and short and can be applied at the market, industry or subindustry level. The three different neutralization methods determine which groups are used for neutralizing alpha values. 
Neutralization is done by subtracting the mean of alpha values of a group (subindustry, industry or the entire market) from each of the alpha values for that particular group. After neutralization, the values of the longs and shorts in the alpha are equal and should be more resistant to market fluctuations or crises.

We highly discourage you from developing no neutralization alphas, as they are highly prone to market fluctuations and risky to trade. We would recommend you use some form of neutralization as this helps to hedge market risk, and helps reduce large drawdowns. The correct choice of neutralization depends on the logic or formula used by the alpha. The results should indicate which neutralization will be most effective.
