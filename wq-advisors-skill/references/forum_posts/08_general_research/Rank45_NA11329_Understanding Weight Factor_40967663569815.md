## 帖子正文

Has anyone figured out the best way to consistently grow their Weight Factor (WF)?

I've noticed something strange with mine recently. In the beginning, it was increasing steadily, which made sense as I continued submitting and improving alphas. But lately, it seems to be increasing and decreasing at the same time, even during periods when I feel my research quality has improved.

I'm trying to better understand what drives sustained WF growth versus what causes it to decline.

A few questions for those with more experience:

• What has contributed the most to increasing your WF over time?
• Are there common mistakes that cause WF to drop unexpectedly?
• How much do submission quality, acceptance rate, combined performance, and consistency influence WF?
• Is it better to focus on fewer high-quality alphas or maintain a higher submission volume?

I'd love to hear how others approach this. My goal is to build a more consistent process for accumulating WF while avoiding the fluctuations that seem hard to explain.
