## 帖子正文

Hello everyone,
My submitted alpha satisfies both conditions: regular alpha and power pool alpha. However, I still need to describe the alpha and have the Power Pool Correlation index.
I would like to ask: if after submitting the alpha, I uncheck the PowerPoolSelected tag, will the Power Pool Correlation index still be counted?
I look forward to receiving everyone's opinions, thank you!

---

## 讨论与评论 (1)

### 评论 #1 (作者: NM12321, 时间: 9个月前)

When you remove the PowerPool Alpha tag, the PPA correlation is still calculated. The PowerPool Alpha tag will count the points for the alphas in your competition.
