## 帖子正文

Could you please clarify the scoring methodology for MAPC? I submitted three alphas, each showing a comparison change above 4k, but my leaderboard score increased by only 7k in total. All three alphas meet the competition’s stated criteria, so I would appreciate your guidance on what factors might explain the discrepancy.

---

## 讨论与评论 (1)

### 评论 #1 (作者: CM46415, 时间: 2个月前)

MAPC scoring is adjusted for overlap, risk, and normalization. Even if individual alphas show large gains, leaderboard impact reflects marginal contribution after redundancy filtering and scaling, not simple additive performance totals.
