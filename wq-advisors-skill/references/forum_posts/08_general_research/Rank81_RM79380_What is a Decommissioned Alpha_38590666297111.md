# What is a Decommissioned Alpha?

## 帖子正文

an  *alpha*  is a predictive signal for future returns. A  **decommissioned alpha**  is one that has been retired and removed from active use.

Common reasons include:

- **Performance decay**  (falling Sharpe or predictive power)
- **High correlation**  with existing alphas (low diversification value)
- **Market changes**  making the signal ineffective
- **Overfitting or failing internal risk/quality filters**

**Takeaway:**  Alpha research isn’t just about creation—it’s about continuous validation. If a signal loses edge or uniqueness, it’s better retired than carried.
