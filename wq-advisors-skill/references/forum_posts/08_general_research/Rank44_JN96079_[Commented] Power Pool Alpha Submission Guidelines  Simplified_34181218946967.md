### 1.  **Qualifying for Monthly Leaderboard Awards**

- To be eligible for monthly awards in any of the  **8 Power Pool leaderboards** , you must have at least  **10 tagged Alphas**  in that leaderboard.
- **No minimum number of submissions per month**  is required.
- Bonus: These 10 Alphas can have been submitted  **at any time** —even before the month in question.

### 2.  **Submission Limits After Three Months**

Once you've been submitting Power Pool Alphas for  **3 months** , the following caps kick in:

- **Monthly Limit**
  You can submit up to  **10 “pure” Power Pool Alphas**  per  *calendar month* .
  - These are Alphas that are  **only**  Power Pool entries—not also classified as  **Atom**  or  **Regular**  Alphas.
- **Daily Limit**
  You may submit up to  **1 pure Power Pool Alpha per day** .
- **Note** : Alphas classified as  **[Power Pool + ATOM]**  or  **[Power Pool + Regular]**   **do not count**  toward these monthly or daily limits.

### **In Summary**

1. **Earn Awards** : You need just  **10 tagged Alphas per leaderboard** —no requirement to submit them within any specific month, even if they were submitted earlier.
2. **After 3 Months of Participation** :
   - Submit up to  **10 pure Power Pool Alphas per month** .
   - Submit at most  **1 pure Power Pool Alpha per day** .
   - Submissions tagged as  **Power Pool + ATOM**  or  **Power Pool + Regular**  don’t count toward these limits.
