## 帖子正文

Since Power Pool alpha submissions are limited to one per day, and this competition requires multiple child alphas (which are part of the Power Pool) that do not introduce new operators or data fields meaning they don’t influence tie-breaker criteria. my question is:  **will these child Power Pool alphas still affect the Combined Alpha Performance, Combined Selected Alpha Performance, Combined Power Pool Alpha Performance, or the VF and WF metrics?**

---

## 讨论与评论 (3)

### 评论 #1 (作者: 顾问 PN39025 (Rank 87), 时间: 8个月前)

The daily limit is 4 alphas. If it is outside of these 4 alphas, these sub-alphas will only be counted for AIA and will not be counted for any other criteria later.

---

### 评论 #2 (作者: SS35873, 时间: 8个月前)

No, they won't affect your metrics if you submit those beyond the quota of 4 daily alphas.

---

### 评论 #3 (作者: AG14039, 时间: 6个月前)

The daily submission limit is four alphas; anything submitted beyond those four will count only toward AIA and won’t be considered for any other evaluation criteria afterward.
