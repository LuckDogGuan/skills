Hello,

To progress to a higher level in the Genius Program, there are a few key steps to follow:

1. **First Meet the Eligibility Criteria** : Ensure you fulfill the requirements for the desired level. Once you achieve this, submitting additional signals or pyramids will no longer contribute to your rank advancement, as these are solely for meeting the qualification criteria.
2. **Continuous Focus on Alpha Creation** : While working toward eligibility, prioritize creating high-quality alpha by leveraging diverse data fields and operators. Remember, using a wider range of data fields and operators increases your chances of success. However, there's a catch: keep the number of operators and fields per alpha as low as possible to maintain efficiency.  **This is the most important part.**
3. **Engage with the Community** : Stay active in the community by sharing posts and comments. Active engagement can positively impact your progress.
