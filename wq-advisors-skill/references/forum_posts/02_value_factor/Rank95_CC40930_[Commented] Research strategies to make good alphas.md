### 评论 #1 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

Operators such as rank, power, tail, etc., cut extreme alpha values. Use these operators to improve alpha research.

---

### 评论 #2 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

To assess the robustness of an alpha, use the rank operator to see if the performance of the Alpha is still good. This is because the rank operator will reveal whether the backtested PnL is generated by only a few stocks.

---

### 评论 #3 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

Don't try to improve the sharpe ratio of your alpha just by tweaking parameters. Your alpha should make logical sense both from a mathematical and economic perspective.

---

### 评论 #4 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

To avoid overfitting, select parameters that are applicable across different stock pools.

Do not try to connect two weak signals to create a stronger signal.

---

### 评论 #5 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

To understand how often a data field is updated, try simulating the data field itself. If it generates a high turnover rate, it means that that field is updated more frequently.

---

### 评论 #6 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

Large decay values ​​can negatively impact the performance of your alpha. Avoid using decay just to make a single alpha appear to have a higher return. Instead, use a reasonable number of decay days for your alpha to avoid impacting its performance.

---

### 评论 #7 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

Do not unnecessarily filter out stocks from your alpha by adding multiple conditions or assigning NaN values ​​to stocks.

---

### 评论 #8 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

How to search efficiently on the data explorer page -

- Adjust your keywords, or try to see if you can find what you are looking for more precisely.
- Reverse search - Search again in reverse order through the datafield name or description that has been found.
- Use abbreviations: For example, Price to Earnings Ratio → PE, or Implied Volatility → IV.

---

### 评论 #9 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

Hoping to improve performance by constantly adjusting settings has a dangerous tendency of overfitting. Please consider starting from ideas and improving alpha's performance first.

---

### 评论 #10 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)

When making an alpha, the first question to think about is: How does each backtest setting affect the performance of my alpha?

### 评论 #11 (作者: AT56452, 时间: 1年前)
