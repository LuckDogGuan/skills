It was mentioned during the June Opportunity webinar that  **Power Pool alphas submitted after the competition deadline are being disregarded** .

This raises a few questions I think many of us would benefit from clarifying:

- **What does “disregarded” actually mean here?**  Are these alphas completely excluded from any evaluation? Or is there some threshold where high performing ones might still be considered?

If anyone has more clarity on this or can share key takeaways from the webinar, it would be really helpful. A lot of us are trying to align our strategies with the current direction. Thanks in advance!
