## 帖子正文

The stock market is essentially a marketplace where investors buy and sell shares of publicly listed companies. These shares, also known as stocks or equities, represent partial ownership in a business. When you invest in a company’s stock, you’re betting on its growth and future profitability. Prices fluctuate based on supply and demand, company performance, and broader economic factors. Overall, the stock market plays a key role in helping businesses raise capital while giving investors opportunities to grow their wealth.
