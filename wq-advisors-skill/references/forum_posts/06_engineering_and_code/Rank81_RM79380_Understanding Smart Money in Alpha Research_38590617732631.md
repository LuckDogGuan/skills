## 帖子正文

*Smart Money*  isn’t a formal product—it’s a research theme focused on capturing the behavior of informed or institutional investors.

These signals often leverage:

- **Insider activity**  (legal buys/sells)
- **Institutional positioning**  (e.g., holdings changes)
- **Volume or flow anomalies**  ahead of key events like earnings

The idea is simple: if informed capital leaves footprints, systematic signals can detect and follow them.

In practice, Smart Money alphas aim to translate  **information advantage into persistent return signals**  by modeling where sophisticated investors are likely positioning.
